Arcana Invoker: Chronological Farming Guide

Phase 1: Early Game & Reputation (Preparation)
Before you begin the specific item hunts, you must ensure your character has the following ranks.
Rank 10 Good: Required for BattleMage Armor.
Rank 10 Chaos: Required for Chaotic Healer.
Rank 10 Arcangrove: Required for Arcangrove Blueprint.
Rank 10 Doomwood: Required for Legion Necromancer items.

Phase 3: Darkon Saga & End-Game Progression

Items 11 through 21 primarily focus on the Darkon/Astravia storyline and high-level boss drops.

11 - Strength’s Fortitude
Enchanted Martial Artist's Gi: /join superslayin — Merge item. Requires Martial Artist's Gi (dropped by Ryoku in /dragonkoi), Fatal Lily x25, Pockey Ball x25, and Dragon Orb x10.
Strong Axe of Golmoth: /join dreampalace — Merge item. Requires Axe of Golmoth (drop from Golmoth), 
Scales of Golmoth x15, and Token of Fire x20.
Fortitude Tonic (x11): /join alchemyacademy — Created via Alchemy. Requires Necrot and Roc Tongue.
Strong Drag’s Intact Wing (x11): /join dracocon — Dropped by the boss Strong Drag.

The Answer: /join battlefowl — Dropped by Zeuster Projection.
Astero’s Insight (x12): /join fortressdelve — Dropped by the boss Astero.

13 - Death’s Mortality
Enchanted DeathKnight: /join bonecastle — Merge item. Requires DeathKnight armor (Rank 10 Doomwood) and 35,000 Gold.
Death Pit Arena Medal (x250): /join deathpit — Dropped by any monster in the map.
Death's Oversight (x10): /join shadowattack — Dropped by Death.
Super Death's Scythe Fragment (x13): /join superdeath — Dropped by Super Death (Warning: Low drop rate, expect a long farm).

14 - Temperance’s Frugality
Celestial Wings of Guiding: /join celestialrealm — Purchase from the merge shop. Requires 100 Infernal Tokens (farmed in /lostruinswar).

Northern Crown (x10): /join snowmore — Dropped by Jon S'Nooooooo.
Azkorath's Wing (x14): /join infernalspire — Dropped by Azkorath.

15 - Devilish Temptation
Fiendish Outlaw: /join fiendpast — Dropped by Fiendish Outlaw.
Fiendish Remains (x50): /join voidrefuge — Complete the "Fiendish Remains" quest from Carnax.
Glass Horns: /join ashfallcamp — Dropped by Smoldur.
Fiend Champion's Spike (x15): /join originul — Dropped by Fiend Champion.

16 - The Tower’s Upheaval
Arcangrove Tower House: /join buyhouse — Purchase for 200,000 Gold (Requires Rank 10 Arcangrove).
Earth Stone (x500): /join fableforest — Complete the "Back to your Roots" quest repeatedly.
Dragon Runestone (x20): /join alchemyacademy — Buy from the merge shop for 100,000 Gold each ($2,000,000$ gold total).
Nevanna’s Revelation (x16): /join gaiazor — Dropped by Nevanna.
