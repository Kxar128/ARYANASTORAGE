  Step 1 — LightMage
  ────────────────────
  → /join celestialrealm  ·  Purchase Evolved LightCaster armour from Aranx (1,000 AC — cannot be sold)
  → Open LightMage Class Token A Shop → Purchase Token A
  → Merge LightMage using Token A

  Step 2 — LightCaster
  ──────────────────────
  → Accept "LightCaster Class" quest from Aranx at /join celestialrealm

  Burning Blade               ×1   → /join lostruinswar  ·  Kill Diabolical Warlord  (~1%)

  Avatar of Death's Scythe    ×1   → Complete Aranx's quest  ·  Kill 500 Underworld Hounds
  Spear of Time               ×1   → Complete Aranx's quest  ·  Kill 500 Infernal Imps
  Aranx's Pure Light          ×1   → /join celestialarena  ·  Kill Aranx  (~10%)
