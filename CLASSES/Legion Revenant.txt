  Worth Getting?
  ──────────────
  Yes — meta support/farming class.
  Note: Dragon of Time and ArchFiend are also great options requiring less time.

  Best Used For:  🌾 Farming  |  🤝 Support
  Also Good For:  ⚔️  Soloing

  Requirements:   Level 80  |  Rank 10 Evil  |  Legion Member
                  Dark Caster Class (to enter the map)
                  → If missing: buy Infinite Legion Dark Caster at /join underworld (2k LTs)

  Non-Member also needs: 50 Bone Dust (from /join battleunderb)

  Recommended:    Paragon Pet (farm LTs in Fealty 3)
                  Undead damage-boosting item (e.g. BLoD) — most enemies are Undead-tagged

  💡 Shortcut:    Trade 1 Dage the Evil Insignia at /join ultradage
                   → 1 Revenant's Spellscroll (Fealty 1 + 3)
                   → 2 Conquest Wreaths (Fealty 2)
                   → 1 Exalted Crown (Fealty 3)
                  Best used on Fealty 1 and 3 — longest and most tedious
