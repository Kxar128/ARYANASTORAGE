╔══════════════════════════════════════╗
║              STANCES                 ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════╝

  There are 9 stances total, but only 6 matter:

  🩹 HoT while active (heals over time)
  ─────────────────────────────────────
  → Zard-Gorilla      (2-3)
  → Zard-Dragon       (2-4)

  ⚡ +20% Crit Chance while active
  ─────────────────────────────────────
  → Gorilla-Zard      (3-2)
  → Gorilla-Dragon    (3-4)

  🛡️ First auto after switching applies Spinning Dragon
     (makes enemy's next hit deal 0 damage)
  ─────────────────────────────────────
  → Dragon-Zard       (4-2)
  → Dragon-Gorilla    (4-3)

  💡 Each skill's buff/debuff is described in its own tooltip.


╔══════════════════════════════════════╗
║           DODGE & EVASION            ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════╝

  Max dodge requires 86.3% evasion or higher
  (includes hit chance debuffs on enemy)

  MA base evasion:   71.85%
  With Dragon-Zard:  91.85% effective dodge after 2 autos  ✅ Max dodge

     You're effectively infinite mana while it's active,
     so it's also a good window to squeeze in more DPS.

  💡 Dragon-Zard (4-2) can be slotted in anywhere to
     refresh max dodge without breaking your flow.
