Overview

Worth Getting?
Generally no because there are better options at Level 80 that cost less or nothing at all. However, it's a good choice if you don't want to farm much as it only costs 1k ACs.

Uses
Good For: Soloing & Support

Enhancement

Requirements

Level 80

1000 ACs

Storyline Completion
This includes /celestialrealm > /lostruins > /lostruinswar and /celestialarena

Quest

LightMage

Purchase the Evolved LightCaster armour from Aranx at /celestialrealm for 1000 ACs. It cannot be sold.

Open the LightMage Class Token A Shop and purchase Token A.

Merge LightMage using Token A.



LightCaster

Once you've got LightMage, you can start the "LightCaster Class" quest from Aranx. To start the quest, it requires: 

Burning Blade
Defeat the Diabolical Warlord at /lostruinswar (1% droprate).

Burning Blade of Azebeth
Defeat Aranx at /celestialarena (5% droprate).

Guardian of Spirits' Blade
Complete Aranx's quest "Guardian of Spirits' Blade" by defeating 500 Fallen Knights.

Avatar of Death's Scythe
Complete Aranx's quest "Avatar of Death's Scythe" by defeating 500 Underworld Hounds.

Spear of Time
Complete Aranx's quest "Spear of Time" by defeating 500 Infernal Imps.

Accept the "LightCaster Class" quest from Aranx at /celestialrealm and obtain Aranx's Pure Light from Aranx at /celestialarena (10% droprate).
