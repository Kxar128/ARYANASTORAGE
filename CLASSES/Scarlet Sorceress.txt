Overview

Worth Getting?
Yes, if you're a low-mid level player who doesn't have ArchFiend or Blaze Binder.

Uses
Good For: Farming

Requirements
Storyline Completion
This includes /hedgemaze > /towerofmirrors.

Level 50

Quest

Obtain Blood Sorceress by defeating Scarletta in /towerofmirrors. To reach her from the starting room, walk left > up > up > up > right > down. If you haven't done the storyline, see the Requirements section, or /goto someone at the boss.
Then, defeat one enemy with Blood Sorceress then turn in the Thief of Hearts' quest "Blood Sorceress to Scarlet Sorceress".

ENHANCEMENTS

Class - Wizard | Luck

Weapon - Elysium | Valiance | Ravenous
Helm - Pneuma | Luck
Cape - Vainglory | Luck

COMBOS

Just stack your 5 skill five times, then use 2, 3, and 4 as they come off cooldown. 5 last for 18 seconds, so you don't have to use this every single time it comes off cooldown when you need to refresh it.
