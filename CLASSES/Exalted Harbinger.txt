COMBOS

4(miss)-5(siphon)-2(crit)-3(heal) -- to deal damage.

5(siphon)-4(miss)-2(crit) -- do it repetitively when you are at a decent chunk of health (e.g. 700-1000). Can be done with or without 5.

4(miss)-3(heal)-3(heal) -- use when you're at low health. If you're more of a risk-taker, try 4(miss)-2(crit)-2(crit)-3(heal)

This should be the combo to usually use: 5-4-2-2-3
