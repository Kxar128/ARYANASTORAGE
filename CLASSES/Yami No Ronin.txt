╔══════════════════════════════════════╗
║               COMBOS                 ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════╝

  ─────────────── DODGE PLAYSTYLE ───────────────

  💡 Dodge Threshold  86–87% dodge = no monster can hit you
  💡 Flash Stacks ···  You gain 3 stacks between each Skill 5 reset
                        Need 21% dodge buffer minimum to stay above threshold
                        Aim for 108% total dodge = 33% shown in char stat panel (press C)

  ✅ Dodge Rotation ·  Press  5  every time it comes off cooldown
                        If you miss the nuke, press  T  to stop attacking (preserves dodge)
                        Spam  2  for extra damage  |  Use  3  if you have mana
                        ❌ Never use  4  — you should never need the heal

  ─────────────── OFFENSIVE ROTATION ────────────

  💡 Skill 4 ········  Use as much as possible to generate flash stacks faster (= more damage)
  💡 Skill 3 ········  After using it, wait 3–4 seconds before stacking Skill 2
                        (This is why most combos go 3→4→4→2 — two Skill 4s bridge the timing)
  💡 DoT Ticks ······  5 total ticks  |  1 second between the 5th tick and the effect fading
                        Time your nuke (Skill 5) for that window

  ✅ Offensive ······  3 → 4 → 4 → 2  →  spam  2  and  4  to build stacks
                        → Nuke with  5  after the 5th DoT tick
                        ⚠️ Watch global cooldown — better to nuke on time than rush and lose damage
