Quest

Non-Member
Complete the 13 Lords of Chaos story (open the Map then click on Story) then farm Rank 10 Chaos. After, purchase the class at /mountdoomskull. 

Member
Members don't need to complete the 13 Lords of Chaos story - just go to /mountdoomskull, farm Rank 10 Chaos, and press Legend Skip at Loremaster Maya. Then purchase the class.
