King's Echo Class Obtain Guide

Prerequisites:
- Level 80
- Completion of the Rumbling of Cold Thunder saga
- Rank 10 in Good faction
- Rank 10 in Swordhaven faction
- Alden’s Liberation Armor
- Completion of “The Storm Queen” content

Starting Location:
- Go to /terminatemple and talk to Victoria (right side)

Questline Steps:

1. Familial Blessing
- Click on Tara on screen 1 in /terminatemple

2. Echo of the King
You must obtain the following items (quests provided by Victoria):
- Alteon’s Reforged Armor
- King Alteon’s Crown
- Royal Dragon Sword
- Alden’s Mace
  Note: Alden’s Mace can be picked up at /castle. Enter the throne room, turn right, and take it from the wall. No quest required.

3. The Path to the King (Sword Quest)
Location: /portal

Requirements:
- Endured Against the Defiler x100
  - Destructive Defiler at /infernalarena (1 per kill)
  - Member option: Graveclaw at /gravechallenge (10 per kill)

- Endured Against the Monster King x90
  - Dethrix at /ebondungeon

- Endured Against a Fallen Friend x100
  - Ultra Sepulchure at /sepulchurebattle

- Endured Against Chaos x113
  - Ultra Chaos Warlord at /chaosboss

- Endured Against the Great Dragon x70
  - Final dragon at /naoisegrave

4. Purify the Crown (Crown Quest)
Location: /cleric

Requirements:
- Trace of Chaos x13
  - Ultra Drakath at /ultradrakath (low drop)
  - OR merge using 5x Drakath Insignia at /championdrakath

- Whispers of Chaos x40
  - Ultra Dreadhaven General at /ultradread
  - OR Slugwrath at /dreadhaven

- Calcified Tear x60
  - Queen of Monsters at /transformation (chance drop)
  - OR guaranteed at /queenbattle

5. The King's Armour

Requirements:
- 500K Gold Voucher x100
  - Farm at /sevencircleswar (F2P)
  - OR /lichwar (Member)
  - OR gold farming via Leery

- Dwarven Gold x30
  - Dwarves at /thelimacity

- Atlas Gold x75
  - King Zedek at /atlasfalls
  - OR Atlas Lions

- Skye Gold x50
  - Queen Iona at /queeniona (approximately 25 kills)

- Camlan Gold x120
  - Two elven minibosses near the end of /camlan

Completion:
After completing all quests and requirements, you will unlock and obtain the King’s Echo class.
