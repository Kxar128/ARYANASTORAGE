⚔️ QUESTS
╭──────── EVERYONE ────────╮
  Complete Iadoa's 13LoC chapter (Map > Story)
  --> /join thespan > /join timelibrary > /join timevoid > /join aqlesson
      > /join dflesson > /join mqlesson > /join deepchaos > /join timespace
╰──────── MEMBER ────────╮
  1. Complete Malorie's "ChronoSpan Membership Dues" quest from /join collection
     ⚠️ NOTE: This quest gives 15k Rep for this faction (normally 5k)

  2. Accept Drakath (Kid)'s "For Science" quest from /join terrarium

  3. Join /join collectorlab and defeat enemies there
       Token Cs have a 70% drop rate and stack to 500

  4. Merge Token Cs --> Token Bs --> Token As
       in the Super Fan Token shop at /join collection

  Rank 1–10 requires ×9 Token As with consumable Rep Boost + Cape of Awe
  (or other 25% boost)

  💡 TIP: Enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In
     from Advanced Options (press L to open Quest Log)

  👥 GROUP: Complete Warlic's "Defeat Ultra Iadoa" quest from /join ultravoid
     Get a Dragonoid of Hours from Dragonoid at /join mqlesson — disables
     Iadoa's Astral Shift (just keep it in inventory, no need to equip)
     This quest also rewards a character page badge
╰──────── NON-MEM ────────╮
  Option 1: Complete Mince's "Do the 'do" quest from /join thespan
  --> From Warlic (start of map): walk left > up > left > up to reach Mince
  --> Defeat the Moglin Ghost outside Mince's shop
      and the Tog & Minx Fairy just to the right

  Option 2: Complete Kairos' "Time is running out..." quest from /join portalwar
  --> Defeat Tempus Larvae in the same map
  --> Use the screen upwards from the fountain and Neverborn (no moving around needed)
