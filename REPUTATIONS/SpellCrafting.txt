       SPELL CRAFTING

╚════════════════════════════════╝

📖 OVERVIEW
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  SpellCrafting is located at /join dragonrune
  Talk to Neveya behind the 4th door (blue rune)
  Complete "You'll need a Spellbook!" to get started:
  --> Click the blue arrows in the room behind you
  --> Defeat Skeletal Warriors at /join castleundead
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

🌿 REAGENTS
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Arcane Quills      --> Purchase from Neveya with Gold Vouchers (easiest method)

  If not spending Gold, obtain the following instead:

  Mystic Quills      --> Defeat Slugfit at /join mobius (walk up > right)
                         OR obtain while completing "Nulgath (Larvae)"
                         Used for: Ember, Radiant, Runic, Fading, Elemental Ink

  Mystic Parchment   --> Defeat Blade Masters and the Undead Legend at /join underworld
                         Used for: Hallow and Zealous Ink

  Mystic Shards      --> Defeat Astral Ephemerites at /join timespace
                         (walk right to find 3) ~15% drop rate
                         OR defeat Grief at /join underrealm
                         (keep walking right) ~50% drop rate
                         Used for: Archmage Ink

  Once you have the scroll you want to make, merge the correct ink
  then join /join spellcraft
  Combinations for each scroll can be found on the AQW Wiki
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

📜 USEFUL SCROLLS
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Scroll of Ethereal Slumber  --> Used for ArchPaladin
                                  Use Archmage Ink

  Scroll of Underworld        --> Used for Void Paladin
                                  Use Archmage Ink

  Scroll of Enrage            --> Used for Ultra Bosses · Stacks to ×160
                                  Use Zealous Ink

  ⚠️ NOTE: Do NOT use scrolls in combat — use Alchemy consumables instead
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

⬆️ RANKING UP
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  1. Purchase from Neveya's Buy Arcane Quills shop:
     --> ×2 Gold Voucher 500k
     --> ×8 Gold Voucher 100k
     (See the Gold guide if you're low on Gold)
  2. Trade for ×18 Arcane Quills
  3. Open AC Get Spell Ink shop and buy ×50 Entropic Ink
     Use leftover Quills to buy more Entropic Ink if needed (stacks to 50)
  4. /join spellcraft > press Begin > choose Entropic Ink
  5. Drag each matching symbol from the left into the corresponding slot
  6. Repeat until Rank 10
