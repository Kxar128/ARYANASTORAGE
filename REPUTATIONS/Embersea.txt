╰──────── MEMBER ────────╮
  1. Complete the storyline from /join brimestone > /join nightmare
  2. Complete Malorie's "Embersea Membership Dues" quest from /join collection
  3. Accept Drakath (Kid)'s "For Science" quest from /join terrarium
  4. Join /join collectorlab and defeat enemies there
       Token Cs have a 70% drop rate and stack to 500
  5. Merge Token Cs --> Token Bs --> Token As
       in the Super Fan Token shop at /join collection

  Rank 1–10 requires ×25 Token As with consumable Rep Boost + Cape of Awe
  (or other 25% boost)

  💡 TIP: Enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In
     from Advanced Options (press L to open Quest Log)
╰──────── NON-MEM ────────╮
  If "Where There's Smoke..." is still available (Blazebalm's quests not finished):
  --> /join feverfew > walk left > up from Blazebalm and repeatedly farm it

  If Blazebalm's quests are done:
  👥 GROUP: Complete Pyralis' "Defeat Mega Tyndarius" quest from /join lavarun
            Requires completing the entire Embersea storyline

  Solo: Complete High Adept Caustus' "Slay the Blazebinders" quest from /join fireforge
  --> Defeat the Blazebinder in the same map (take the left path)
╰──────── DAILIES ────────╮
