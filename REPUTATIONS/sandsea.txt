Uses

Classes
Master Ranger, Legendary Elemental Warrior.

Quests

Everyone

Complete Tibicenas' 13LoC chapter (Map > Story). This includes /sandsea > /sandport > /pyramid > /wanders > /sandcastle > /djinn.



Member

Complete Malorie's "Sandsea Membership Dues" quest from /collection. Normally this quest rewards 5k Rep but for this faction it gives 15k.

Accept Drakath (Kid)'s "For Science" quest from /terrarium.

Join /collectorlab and defeat enemies there. Token Cs have a 70% drop rate and stack to 500.

Merge them for Token Bs > Token As in the Super Fan Token shop at /collection.

Getting from Rank 1-10 for this faction requires 9 Token As with a consumable Rep Boost and Cape of Awe (or other 25% boost).

Also make sure to enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In from Advanced Options. This way you can keep your Quest Log open and turn in quests from there. You can access your Quest Log by pressing L.



Non-Mem

Accept Zhoom's "Dissertations Burpers Camel" and "Crafting Creepers: A Favourite of Mine", Cassim's "Oasis Ornaments", Nkuku's "Parched Pets", and Kephri's "The Power of Pomade" quests from /sandsea. Complete them by defeating Cactus Creepers and the Bupers Camel in the same map - they are on separate screens but next to each other.
Also make sure to enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In from Advanced Options. This way you can keep your Quest Log open and turn in quests from there. You can access your Quest Log by pressing L.
