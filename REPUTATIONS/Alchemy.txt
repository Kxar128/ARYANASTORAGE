         ALCHEMY

╚════════════════════════════════╝

📖 OVERVIEW
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Alchemy is a minigame where you create consumables:
  Potions, Tonics, and Elixirs (all 3 may be called Pots or Drugs)
  Equipped and used from your 6th slot.

  🧪 Potions/Philtres  --> Short duration (15-45s) · Use IN combat
                           Boosts: Haste, Evasion, Crit Chance, HP/MP

  🧴 Tonics            --> Long duration (up to 15m) · Use BEFORE combat
                           Boosts: STR, DEX, INT, END, WIS, LCK

  ⚗️  Elixirs           --> Mix of both · Long duration, boosts secondary stats

  Versions: Basic (weaker) · Regular · Potent (stronger) · Unstable (stronger
  but with a drawback). With a Dragon Runestone: Potions/Elixirs = Potent or
  Unstable; Tonics = Regular or Unstable.

⭐ BEST CONSUMABLES
╭──────── ALL MIXED CONSUMABLES REQUIRE RANK 8 ALCHEMY (GEBO RUNE) ────────╮

  🗡️ Lucky classes   --> Battle Elixir + Fate Tonic + Felicitous Philtre
  🔮 Wizard classes  --> Malevolence Elixir + Fate Tonic + Felicitous Philtre

  💡 NOTE: Felicitous Philtre vs Potent Honour depends on class and preference
     Use Felicitous Philtre if crit rate is below ~87% or class uses DoT
     Use Potent Honour if you can afford the mana debuff and crit is ~87%+

  ─── ELIXIRS ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Battle Elixir        --> Increases Physical Dmg by 15/20/25% for 15m
    Potent:  4× Gold Voucher 500k = 8 Elixirs
             OR mix Doomatter + Nimblestem
    Regular: 4× Gold Voucher 500k = 10 Elixirs
             OR mix Dragon Scale + Rhison Blood

  Malevolence Elixir   --> Increases Spell Power by 15/20/25% for 15m
    Potent:  4× Gold Voucher 500k = 8 Elixirs
             OR mix Doomatter + Necrot/Nimblestem
    Regular: 4× Gold Voucher 500k = 10 Elixirs
             OR mix Doomatter + Dried Slime

  ─── TONICS ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Fate Tonic           --> Increases Luck by 20% for 15m
    Unstable reduces Endurance by 10%
    Unstable: 4× Gold Voucher 500k = 6 Tonics
              OR mix Dried Slime + Trollola Nectar
    Regular:  4× Gold Voucher 500k = 10 Tonics
              OR mix Arashtite Ore/Dried Slime/Trollola Nectar + any reagent

  Sage Tonic           --> Increases Intelligence by 20% for 15m
    Unstable reduces Endurance by 10%
    Unstable: 2× Gold Voucher 500k = 6 Tonics
              OR mix Chaoroot + Chaos Entity
    Regular:  2× Gold Voucher 500k = 10 Tonics
              OR mix Arashtite Ore + Doomatter/Necrot/Dragon Scale

  Body Tonic           --> Increases Endurance by 20% for 15m
    Unstable reduces Luck by 10%
    Unstable: 2× Gold Voucher 500k = 6 Tonics
              OR mix Nimblestem + Roc Tongue
    Regular:  2× Gold Voucher 500k = 10 Tonics
              OR mix Roc Tongue + any other reagent

  Might Tonic          --> Increases Strength by 20% for 15m
    Unstable reduces Endurance by 10%
    Unstable: 2× Gold Voucher 500k = 6 Tonics
              OR mix Chaos Entity + Fish Oil
    Regular:  2× Gold Voucher 500k = 10 Tonics
              OR mix Dragon Scale + Rhison Blood

  ─── POTIONS ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

  Honour Potion        --> Increases Dmg by 50/75%, Mana Cost +15% for 25/35s
    Requires Rank 10 Good
    Potent:  1× Gold Voucher 500k = 5 Potions
             OR mix Chaos Entity + Fish Oil
    Regular: 2× Gold Voucher 100k = 25 Potions
             OR mix Chaoroot + Necrot/Dragon Scale/Dried Slime/Rhison Blood

  Felicitous Philtre   --> Increases Luck by 50% for 45s
    2× Gold Voucher 100k = 25 Potions · Cannot be mixed

  Potion of Evasion    --> Increases Evasion by 50% and Haste by 15% for 15s
    Purchase from Reens' at /join arcangrove
    Or trade 2× Gold Voucher 100k for 25 Fleet Foot Philtres (higher stacking)

⚙️ MINIGAME GUIDE
╭──────── RUNES ────────╮
  💡 TIP: Trade Gold Vouchers for Dragon Runestones at Alina's Get Dragon
     Runes shop at /join alchemyacademy — skips all minigame mechanics.

  Join /join alchemy. If using Dragon Runestone --> Select 1st Rune (+Bonus rep)
  Otherwise use the rune matching your target potion's level:

  Jera  --> Level 5–15   · Unlocks at Rank 1
  Uruz  --> Level 16–25  · Unlocks at Rank 3
  Fehu  --> Level 26–40  · Unlocks at Rank 5
  Gebo  --> Level 41+    · Unlocks at Rank 8
╰──────── MECHANICS ────────╮
  Goal: Fill empty potion bottle by balancing the heat.
  Aim for 3 green circles output (2 minimum) · Keep temp at ~70–80°

  🔴 Red button   --> Increases heat (fireballs regenerate)
  🔵 Blue button  --> Freezes runestone to prevent overheat (icicles DO NOT regen)

  ⚠️ NOTE: Harder runes overheat more easily. Sometimes fireballs miss and set the
     room on fire (meter to 100) — unavoidable. Ice may also fail and drop meter
     to -30. If either happens, just leave — you won't lose reagents on leave or fail.
╰──────── RANKING UP ────────╮
  Obtain ×30 Chaoroot and ×30 Necrot from Swindle's Ripoff Emporium at
  /join tercessuinotlim. Use with Dragon Runestones or your highest rune.
