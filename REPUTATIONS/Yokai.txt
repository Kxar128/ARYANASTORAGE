╰──────── MEMBER ────────╮
  Rank 1–3
  Complete Announcer Guy's quests at /join dragonkoiz
  --> Accept them, then select Battle
  --> Repeat "Best Challenge" for farming
  ⚠️ NOTE: Do NOT select Continue — it will throw you out of the room

  💡 TIP: Enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In
     from Advanced Options (press L to open Quest Log)

  Rank 4–10
  Complete Neko Mata's "Who is the Greatest?" quest from /join yokaigrave
  --> Defeat the Level 19 O-dokuro at /join odokuro
  ⚠️ NOTE: Requires completing Kitsune's 13LoC chapter up to /join odokuro
  💡 The Rank 1–3 method above also works here if you prefer — easier for lower levels

  Alternative (skip both methods):
  1. Complete Malorie's "Yokai Membership Dues" quest from /join collection
     ⚠️ NOTE: This quest gives 15k Rep for this faction (normally 5k)
  2. Accept Drakath (Kid)'s "For Science" quest from /join terrarium
  3. Join /join collectorlab and defeat enemies there
       Token Cs have a 70% drop rate and stack to 500
  4. Merge Token Cs --> Token Bs --> Token As
       in the Super Fan Token shop at /join collection

  Rank 1–10 requires ×9 Token As with consumable Rep Boost + Cape of Awe
  (or other 25% boost)

  💡 TIP: Enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In
     from Advanced Options (press L to open Quest Log)
