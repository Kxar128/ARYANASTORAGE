⚔️ QUESTS
╭──────── MEMBER + NON-MEM ────────╮
  Accept the following quests from the Quest Board at /join dreadrock
  (located left of Kaiser Vance in the 2nd room):

  --> "The Scythe's Edge"          --> "Ghastly Blades"
  --> "Forged in Blood"            --> "Glub, Glub, Glub"
  --> "The Abyss Stares Back"      --> "Knife to the Back"
  --> "The Coast is Clear"         --> "Endurance Tesssssst"

  Legion member:  Also accept "Undead Champion Recruitment"
                  Requires Undead Champion armour (buy with Gold from
                  Dage's Legion shop at /join underworld)
  Non-Legion:     Accept "Recruiting for the Undead Legion" instead
                  ⚠️ Don't accept both — drops overlap and Legion quest is superior

  Also accept "Dreadrock Gem Exchange" — requires an Unidentified 13
  (see the Reagent Library if unsure how to obtain one)

  Once all quests are accepted:
  --> Walk upwards from the Quest Board and defeat enemies there
  --> Leave Ghastly Dreadrock Blades in the Custom Drop UI (Advanced Options)
  --> Turn in quests each time you have 50 and 100 of them

  ⚠️ NOTE: Many temporary items drop from this method and may cause lag
     Accept fewer quests if performance becomes an issue

  💡 TIP: Enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In
     from Advanced Options (press L to open Quest Log)

  ─────────────────────────────────
  GOLD METHOD (skip the grind):

  1. Accept "Make a Donation" from the Quest Board at /join dreadrock
  2. Walk left and open Deathgore's (fish bowl) shop
  3. Each Dreadrock Donation Receipt = 2,500 Rep

  Cost to Rank 1–10:
    No boosts:                         ~121m Gold
    Consumable boost + Cape of Awe:     ~49m Gold
    + Active server boost on top:       ~25m Gold

  💡 Check when server boosts occur at the usual community resources
