Uses

Cosmetics Only.

Quests

Non-Mem + Member

Rank 1-4

Accept Shamrock Fairy's "Gain the Sight" quest from /faegrove. Complete it by defeating Lucky Harms at /rainbow (follow the rainbow path to find him).

Rank 5-7

Accept Shamrock Fairy's "Oak for Luck and Joy" quest then defeat Acornents near the end of the same map.

Rank 8-10

Once you're Rank 8, go through the right door from the Acornents (if it doesn't let you enter, leave and come back to this room). Then complete the stone's "Future Sight" quest by defeating Dark Sylphdrakes (2 in the same room can be found near the start of the map).
