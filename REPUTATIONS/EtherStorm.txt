         ETHERSTORM

╚════════════════════════════════╝

🏆 USES
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Class --> Elemental Dracomancer
  💡 Non-mem method also earns Dragonslayer and Dragonslayer General
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

⚔️ QUESTS
╭──────── EVERYONE ────────╮
  Complete the storyline:
  --> /join firestorm > /join airstorm > /join waterstorm > /join earthstorm
      > /join dragonhame > /join dragonheart
╰──────── MEMBER ────────╮
  1. Accept Mino's "Clash to a Cure" and "Sulfur Pure" quests from /join airstorm
     (keep walking right from the beginning — they look like a cat person)
  2. Accept Hs'Sakar's "Something Stinks" quest from /join firestorm
  3. Accept Il'Lar's "Too Hot To Handle" quest from /join dragonhame
     (quick access: exit through the lava spiral portal from /join firestorm)
  4. Defeat Sulfur Imps and Living Fire at /join firestorm
     (quick access: exit left from /join airstorm)
     Farm across the two rooms with 2 of each enemy for best speed,
     or stay on a screen with 1 of each if you prefer not to move around

  💡 TIP: Enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In
     from Advanced Options (press L to open Quest Log)
╰──────── NON-MEM ────────╮
  1. Purchase Dragonslayer from Galanoth's DragonSlayer Rewards shop at /join lair
  2. Get Dragonslayer to Rank 10 by farming Frost Spirits at /join icestormarena
     Stand between the two close enemies at bottom-right
     Enable Freeze/Lock Monster Position from Advanced Options
     ⚠️ NOTE: Abilities 2 and 5 only activate against Dragon enemies — skip them

  3. Go to /join dragontown and accept:
     --> Galanoth's "DragonSlayer Farming Quest" (under Quests)
     --> Galanoth's "Dragonslayer General Class" quest
         (under Dragonslayer General Class > Become a Dragonslayer General)
     --> Pyrik's "Dragon Defender Farming Quest"

  4. Clear all enemies in the map excluding:
     --> The right path
     --> Anything past the 1st room on the left path
     --> Chaos Fluffy
     ⚠️ NOTE: This clear resets on relog

  Efficient route:
  ① Walk up > left from Galanoth — clear until you have a Deluge Draco
    and Dragonslayer kill
  ② Walk right — get a Chaos Draco and Dragonslayer General kill
  ③ Head upwards — clear every enemy twice
  ④ Walk left — clear until all 3 quests are done (~2–3 full room clears)

  💡 TIP: Enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In
     from Advanced Options (press L to open Quest Log)

  ⭐ Save ×75 Enchanted Scales and ×100 Dragon Claws for Dragonslayer General
     Merge at Galanoth when ready
