⚔️ QUESTS
╭──────── EVERYONE ────────╮
  Complete the DoomWood storyline:
  --> /join lightguard > /join doomwood > /join maul > /join necrotower
      > /join necrou > /join temple > /join shadowfallwar > /join lab
      > /join mountain > /join vordredboss > /join necrodungeon
      > /join necrocavern

  Complete the Zombies storyline:
  --> /join doomhaven > /join doomwar
  --> (Member) /join yulgarzombie

  Complete the storyline:
  --> /join thorngarde > /join stonewood > /join techdungeon
      > /join stonewooddeep > /join techfortress

  Obtain BLoD and SDKA (if member)
╰──────── MEMBER ────────╮
  👥 GROUP: Complete GrimDawn/Dusk's "Executive Doom OverLord Challenge"
  quest from /join necrodungeon
  --> Defeat the Doom Overlord in the same map

  Otherwise: Complete Yulgar's "Free Us, Oh Brave One!" quest
  from /join yulgarzombie
  --> Defeat the Dark Dracozombie in the same map
  ⚠️ NOTE: Requires completing Yulgar's previous quests and keeping the reward
     Previous quest enemies are at: /join templeb > /join battleunderb > /join doomwar
╰──────── NON-MEM ────────╮
  👥 GROUP: Complete GrimDawn/Dusk's "Doom OverLord Challenge"
  quest from /join necrodungeon
  --> Defeat the Doom Overlord in the same map

  Otherwise: Accept all three quests from /join shadowfall (click Quests top-right):
  --> "Minion Morale"
  --> "Shadowfall is DOOMed"
  --> "Grave-lyn Danger"
  Accept each separately from each screen, then complete them all in one room
  Recommended room: 2 Undead War Mages + 2 Ghouls

  💡 TIP: Enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In
     from Advanced Options (press L to open Quest Log)
╰──────── DAILIES ────────╮
  From /join necropolis:
  --> Artix's "Mine Crafting" quest (Member + Non-Mem)
  --> Vayle's "Hard Core Metals" quest (Member only)
  Defeat Balboa at /join stalagbite
  Rewards metals used for BLoD, SDKA, and NSoD
