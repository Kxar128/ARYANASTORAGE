Class
Lycan.

Quests

Member

Complete Malorie's "Lycan Membership Dues" quest from /collection.

Accept Drakath (Kid)'s "For Science" quest from /terrarium.

Join /collectorlab and defeat enemies there. Token Cs have a 70% drop rate and stack to 500.

Merge them for Token Bs > Token As in the Super Fan Token shop at /collection.

Getting from Rank 1-10 requires 25 Token As with a consumable Rep Boost and Cape of Awe (or other 25% boost).

Also make sure to enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In from Advanced Options. This way you can keep your Quest Log open and turn in quests from there. You can access your Quest Log by pressing L.



Non-Mem

If you're very low level, farm Faopaw's "Bats of a Leather Flock Together" quest from /lycan. Complete it by defeating Leatherwings at /pines - walk down>right to find 2 alone.
Also make sure to enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In from Advanced Options. This way you can keep your Quest Log open and turn in quests from there. You can access your Quest Log by pressing L.

If you don't have Rank 10 Loremaster, accept Arlen's "Studying the Blood Ancient" quest from /classhall and Constantin's "Sanguine" quest from /lycan. For them, defeat the Albino Bats at /dwarfhold then join /wargrounds and exit upwards to defeat Sanguine. If you do have Rank 10 Loremaster, only farm "Sanguine".
