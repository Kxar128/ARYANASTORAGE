Uses

Cosmetics Only.

Quests

Member + Non-Mem

Accept Haasheth's "Gather Crystals" quest from /dreadfire (they are located at the very left of the map). Then, return to the centre of the map and go through the door in the middle then left to farm the 2 Arcane Crystals.
Also make sure to enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In from Advanced Options. This way you can keep your Quest Log open and turn in quests from there. You can access your Quest Log by pressing L.
