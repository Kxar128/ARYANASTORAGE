Uses

Classes
ArchPaladin, (Classic) Paladin.

Item
CHPA.

Quests

Everyone

Complete the storyline from /manor > /poisonforest and Saa'thel's quests at /mobius.



Alteon's quests require you to be Good-aligned. Change from Evil to Good by turning in a Contract, bought for 100k Gold from him.



Member

Complete Alteon's "Rumble With Grumble" quest from /castle (keep walking upwards to find him) by defeating Grumble at /sewer.
Also make sure to enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In from Advanced Options. This way you can keep your Quest Log open and turn in quests from there. You can access your Quest Log by pressing L.



Non-Mem

Rank 1-3

Complete Alteon's "That Hero Who Chases Slimes" quest from /castle (keep walking upwards to find him) by defeating green Slimes at /swordhavenbridge.
Also make sure to enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In from Advanced Options. This way you can keep your Quest Log open and turn in quests from there. You can access your Quest Log by pressing L.

Rank 4-10

Complete Alteon's "Tomb With A View" quest by defeating Skeletal Warriors and Vikings at /castleundead. This is a lot faster than farming any quests at /poisonforest.

If you don't yet have the Burning Blade weapon, try completing the quests from /celestialrealm > /lostruins > /lostruinswar. Then, farm Aranx's "Defeat the Diabolical Warlord!" quest at /lostruinswar.

Dailies

Member

Complete Alteon's "Brace-r Yourself!" quest from /castle (keep walking upwards to find him) by defeating Cyclops Raiders at /mobius.

If you're above Rank 4, complete "Skele-Tongue" from Alteon by defeating Big Jack Sprat at /graveyard.

Complete Knave1's "Prevent the Return (daily)" quest from /banished by defeating Doomwood Soldiers and Ectomancers at /doomwood. Requires completing his previous quests to access.



Non-Mem

Complete Alteon's "Battle Under Battleon" quest from /castle (keep walking upwards to find him) by defeating Skeletal Warriors and Skeletal Vikings at /castleundead. Complete it alongside "Tomb With A View".
