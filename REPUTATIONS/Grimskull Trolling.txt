   GRIMSKULL TROLLING
        (do it)

╚════════════════════════════════╝

⚔️ QUESTS
╭──────── MEMBER + NON-MEM ────────╮
  /join gaolcell --> Speak to "Undead Cashfear (NPC)" --> Quests

  Accept one of:
  --> "Gaol Rerun"          (750 Rep)
  --> "Gaol Rerun (Legend)" (1,500 Rep)

  Also accept:
  --> "Beat Up My Master For My Enjoyment" (Daily) (2,500 Rep)
      ⚠️ NOTE: Can only be completed once per day
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

🧪 POTIONS REQUIRED
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  ⚠️ Potting is required — Potent Honour Potions interact with Dragon of
     Time's squaring mechanic

  Unstable Sage Tonic          (non-Unstable also works)
  Unstable Malevolence Elixir  (non-Unstable also works)
  Potent Honour / Malice Potion    *** REQUIRED ***
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

⚙️ ENCHANTMENTS
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Weapon --> Elysium / Valiance
  Helm   --> Pneuma / Wizard
  Cape   --> Vainglory / Penitence

  💡 Base Wizard cape enhancement can substitute Penitence if you don't own it
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

🔄 SKILL ROTATION
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Default: 2 → 3 → 5 → 3 → 4 (3)   unless otherwise stated below
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

🗺️ ROOM GUIDE
╭──────── ROOM 1 ────────╮
  Burst before he counters you
  With Elysium / Pneuma / Vainglory + default rotation on low lag:
  --> You can burst him before his counter triggers

  If that doesn't work:
  --> Avoid using (2), use 4 / 5 to reach his first HP threshold
  --> Stop attacking during his counter, then burst him down
  💡 Skill (3) heals you — don't forget it
╰──────── ROOM 2 ────────╮
  Clear with default rotation
╰──────── ROOM 3 ────────╮
  ⚠️ Do NOT be on Vainglory in this room
  When he demands you at full HP:
  --> Avoid pressing 2 / 4 (lowers HP)
  --> Use (3) to stay at max HP
╰──────── ROOM 4 ────────╮
  Clear with default rotation
╰──────── ROOM 5 ────────╮
  Elysium / Pneuma / Penitence is fine here
  If struggling: swap to Wizard helm for more HP
  💡 You'll need Wizard for Room 6 anyway — swapping here is safe
╰──────── ROOM 6 ────────╮
  ⚠️ Wizard helm is MANDATORY here
  The Fell Statue can deal up to 3,000 damage — would 1-shot on Pneuma
╰──────── ROOM 7 ────────╮
  ⚠️ Do NOT be on Vainglory in this room
  When he demands you at full HP:
  --> Avoid pressing 2 / 4 (lowers HP)
  --> Use (3) to stay at max HP
  💡 Pneuma is safe here — just keep max HP% above 50%
╰──────── ROOMS 8–10 ────────╮
  Clear each with default rotation
  ✅ Safe to stay on Pneuma + Vainglory for the rest of the dungeon
     Only 1 more gearswap remains — the hardest parts are done
╰──────── ROOM 11 ────────╮
  Final fight + last gearswap
  1. Let your HoTs fade, then swap to Valiance weapon enhancement
  2. Use 4 / 5 to deal damage and balance (3) to keep HP up

  At 90% HP (900k):
  --> He will Invert Healing for 4 seconds
  --> Wait for it to end, then resume 2 / 3 and default rotation
╰──────── ROOM 12 ────────╮
  Clear the last mob — easy finish
