Uses

Classes
Shaman, Evolved Shaman, StoneCrusher.

Items
EVA, AFDL.

Quests

Everyone

Complete Ledgermayne's 13LoC chapter (Map > Story). This includes /arcangrove > /cloister > /mudluk > /natatorium > /gilead > /mafic > /elemental > /ledgermayne.



Member

Complete Malorie's "Arcangrove Membership Dues" quest from /collection.

Accept Drakath (Kid)'s "For Science" quest from /terrarium.

Join /collectorlab and defeat enemies there. Token Cs have a 70% drop rate and stack to 500.

Merge them for Token Bs > Token As in the Super Fan Token shop at /collection.

Getting from Rank 1-10 requires 25 Token As with a consumable Rep Boost and Cape of Awe (or other 25% boost).

Also make sure to enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In from Advanced Options. This way you can keep your Quest Log open and turn in quests from there. You can access your Quest Log by pressing L.



Non-Mem

Accept Rayst's "A Necessary Sacrifice", Blakk's "Thistle Do Nicely", Moonwrath's "ArcanRobe", and Reens' "Thyme and a Half" quests from /arcangrove.
Complete these quests by defeating Seed Spitters at the bottom of the map (has 3 in the same room).
Also make sure to enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In from Advanced Options. This way you can keep your Quest Log open and turn in quests from there. You can access your Quest Log by pressing L.

Dailies

Complete Reens' "Experiment 107: Elder's Blood Potion" quest from /arcangrove by defeating Gorillaphants. This will reward you with Elders' Blood, which is used for Void Highlord.
Other daily quests aren't worth the effort.
