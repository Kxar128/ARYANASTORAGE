⚔️ QUESTS
╭──────── EVERYONE ────────╮
  Complete the storyline:
  --> /join castle > /join archives > /join armory > /join ceremony
      > /join chaosaltar > /join castleroof > /join swordhavenfalls
      > /join ultraalteon
╰──────── MEMBER ────────╮
  1. Complete Malorie's "Swordhaven Membership Dues" quest from /join collection
  2. Accept Drakath (Kid)'s "For Science" quest from /join terrarium
  3. Join /join collectorlab and defeat enemies there
       Token Cs have a 70% drop rate and stack to 500
  4. Merge Token Cs --> Token Bs --> Token As
       in the Super Fan Token shop at /join collection

  Rank 1–10 requires ×25 Token As with consumable Rep Boost + Cape of Awe
  (or other 25% boost)

  💡 TIP: Enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In
     from Advanced Options (press L to open Quest Log)

  💡 Alternatively use the Non-Mem method below if you prefer not to
     manage the Collector Lab token loop
╰──────── NON-MEM ────────╮
  1. From starting screen walk up and accept Sir Vival's 3 non-daily quests
     and Jessor's "Weapons-in-Progress" quest
  2. Walk up and accept Valerin's quest
  3. Walk left for Nialla's quest
  4. Walk back down > right for Guy's quest
  5. Defeat enemies in Guy's room and the screen to the right
     Go back and forth between them to complete all quests

  💡 TIP: Enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In
     from Advanced Options (press L to open Quest Log)

  👥 GROUP: Farm Lady Celestia's "Defeat Ultra Chaos Lord Alteon" quest
  at /join ultraalteon
  --> Use the Relics of Chaos drops to complete "Supplies to spin The Wheel of Chance"
  ⚠️ NOTE: When the meter appears at top-right, stop attacking or Alteon
     will deal massive damage
