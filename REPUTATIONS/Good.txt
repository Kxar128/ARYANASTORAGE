⚔️ QUESTS
╭──────── MEMBER ────────╮
  Complete Alteon's "Rumble With Grumble" quest from /join castle
  (keep walking upwards to find him)
  --> Defeat Grumble at /join sewer

  💡 TIP: Enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In
     from Advanced Options (press L to open Quest Log)
╰──────── NON-MEM ────────╮
  Rank 1–3
  Complete Alteon's "That Hero Who Chases Slimes" quest from /join castle
  (keep walking upwards to find him)
  --> Defeat green Slimes at /join swordhavenbridge

  💡 TIP: Enable Quest Log Turn-Ins and Reaccept Quest After Turn-In
     from Advanced Options (press L to open Quest Log)

  Rank 4–10
  Complete Alteon's "Tomb With A View" quest
  --> Defeat Skeletal Warriors and Vikings at /join castleundead
  ⚠️ NOTE: Much faster than farming any quests at /join poisonforest

  --> Complete quests from /join celestialrealm > /join lostruins > /join lostruinswar
  --> Then farm Aranx's "Defeat the Diabolical Warlord!" quest at /join lostruinswar
