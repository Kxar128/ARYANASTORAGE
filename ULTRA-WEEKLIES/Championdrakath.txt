     CHAMPION DRAKATH

╚════════════════════════════════╝

⚠️ BOSS MECHANICS
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Every 2,000,000 damage dealt, Drakath screams at your team
  --> Designated taunter MUST taunt JUST BEFORE the 000,000 mark
  --> Each scream grants Drakath +20% Dodge (stacks indefinitely)
  --> Taunting should be done slightly before the 2m cap (2.1m or 6.1m for example
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

⚙️ GEAR SETUPS

📖 DIFFERENT COMPS
╭──────── SAFE COMP ────────╮
Probably slightly longer ultra on this list without a proper comp. 
Requires strict timing and some mana reduction classes. 
Every 2 million HP the boss sends out a nuke and gets stronger, 
it is recommended to taunt this so it hits 1 player instead of all 4 in the party. 
He also gets stronger each time he does the nuke so if a taunt is missed it is recommended to reset. 
It is not required to taunt on 10 million hp.
