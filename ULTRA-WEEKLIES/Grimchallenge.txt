       GRIM CHALLENGE
           (BINKY)

╚════════════════════════════════╝

⚠️ BOSS MECHANICS — HP MILESTONES
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Every 5M HP lost triggers a mechanic
  LR and VDK alternate taunts throughout

  50M  --> Lock designated skills (see class guides below)
  45M  --> Invert Heal · Healers DO NOT heal
  40M  --> MEOW · Must be taunted
  35M  --> BLEEECH · Must be taunted
  30M  --> Warden Mechanic · MUST heal after HP drop
  20M  --> Drakath Mechanic · Deal max DPS
  15M  --> Dage Mechanic · VDK and LR loop Decay and Taunt
  10M  --> Kathool Mechanic · Everyone uses Vigil
           ALL healers spam heals · LH and LoO use skill 5 for survivability
  5M   --> Drakath Mechanic · Final stand — DPS as hard as possible
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

🌀 GENERAL TIPS
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  TAUNTERS    --> Lock a skill before using Taunt

  VIGIL       --> Use Vigil 2–3 seconds after "YOU WILL NOT RESIST" text appears
                  With a fast comp Binky will often be well below 10M HP at this point

  HEALERS     --> Stop spamming heals at 45M HP · Resume after 5 seconds
                  Spam heals again after the HP nuke

  LOCKING     --> Go to /join ultraezrajal or /join binky (member) to lock ManaVamp
                  and swap to a needed weapon enhancement before the fight
                  This preserves all skills and enhancements

⚙️ CLASS GUIDES
╭──────── CHRONO SHADOWSLAYER (CSS) ────────╮
  Lock: 3 or 5 (or pre-lock)
  LUK class · Base Forge helm · Penitence / Vainglory
  Weapon: Arcana Concerto (good ping) / Valiance (bad/mid ping)
  Potions: Unstable Fate Tonic · Unstable Battle Elixir · Feli Pot

  Tips:
  ① During Meow, BLEEECH, Listen, Truth, Drakath, Dage, and Kathool
    mechanics — Binky won't reset rift stacks
    Spam 4 and try to use 5 before the text fades
  ② At fight start, Binky nukes the team (1,350–9,500 dmg spread)
    Wait for this nuke BEFORE attacking
  ③ Control DPS at 35–30M HP in case LR takes the BLEEECH
    LR will likely die from the Warden HP drop as BLEEECH decay is still on them
  ④ If using Feli Pot, try to Gunslinger just as the Kathool Haste debuff hits
    so you can still deal damage while debuffed
╰──────── ARCHFIEND (AF) ────────╮
  Pre-lock weapon enhancement
  LUK class · Base Forge helm · Penitence · Ravenous
  Potions: Fate Tonic · Potent Battle · Vigil

  Tips:
  Use 4 and 5 at 15M HP and spam off cooldown
  Pray CSS can nuke Binky below 5M HP during "You will not Resist" text
╰──────── LEGENDARY HERO (LH) ────────╮
  Lock: 2
  LUK class · Base Forge helm · Absolution · Valiance
  Potions: Fate Tonic · Divine Elixir · Vigil

  Tips:
  ① Use skill 5 when Binky is at 30,500,000 HP so the team survives
    the Warden mechanic
  ② Wait until LoO Quix aura (LoO skill 5) is halfway done before
    pressing 5 at the Kathool mechanic
  ③ DO NOT use skill 5 at 45M HP — your HoT will kill the taunter
  ④ Latest you can use skill 5 is 15M HP
╰──────── LORD OF ORDER (LOO) ────────╮
  Lock weapon enhancement
  LUK class · Base Forge helm · Penitence · Mana Vamp
  Potions: Fate Tonic · Divine Elixir · Vigil

  Tips:
  ① Stop using Quix (skill 5) at 20M HP
  ② Use Quix (skill 5) after HP drops at Kathool mechanic
  ③ Try to use Quix at 45M HP (Heal Invert) so the taunter survives
  ④ Try to use Quix at 30M HP (Warden Mechanic) in case healer
    cooldowns are not ready
  ⚠️ STOP skill 5 at 20M HP · Use Quix → skill 5 at 8M HP
╰──────── ARCHPALADIN (AP) ────────╮
  Lock weapon enhancement
  LUK class · Base Forge helm · Lament · Mana Vamp
  Potions: Unstable Fate Tonic · Unstable Battle · Vigil

  Tips:
  ① Try to Seal (skill 4) at 45M HP (Heal Invert) so the taunter survives
  ② Try to Seal at 35M HP so whoever takes BLEEECH survives
  ③ Heal and Seal as often as possible
    After Heal Invert fades (~43M HP), resume normal rotation
  ④ After Kathool HP drop (~8–7M HP): heal and time Seal for when
    Quix (LoO skill 5) aura is 75% complete
  ⑤ Use AP Seal at 15M HP — your Seal functions as a Decay at this point
  ⚠️ Use skill 4 at 25M HP
╰──────── VERUS DOOMKNIGHT (VDK) ────────╮
  Lock: skill 4
  LUK class · Base Forge helm · Vainglory · Ravenous / Dauntless
  Potions: Fate Tonic · Potent Battle · Scroll of Enrage

  Tips:
  ① On Dauntless: team can heal if you taunt the Heal Invert at 45M HP
    Let the team know if you're taking the Invert on Dauntless
  ② On Dauntless: save Lifesteal skill (skill 3) for the 30M HP mechanic
  ③ On Dauntless: stop spamming 2 during "YOU WILL NOT RESIST"
    so the party can heal you
╰──────── LEGION REVENANT (LR) ────────╮
  Lock: skill 5 (if it locks Arcana Concerto, cry a little inside)
  WIZ class · Healer / Hearty helm · Penitence · Arcana Concerto
  Potions: Sage Tonic · Potent Malevolence · Scroll of Enrage

  Tips:
  ① Stop spamming skill 4 at 47M HP in case you take the Heal Invert
    taunt at 45M HP — resume all skills after Invert fades
  ② Spam skill 2 at 15M HP
