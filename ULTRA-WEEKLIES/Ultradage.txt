📖 ENHANCEMENT BREAKDOWNS
╭──────── DAUNTLESS ────────╮
  100% proc on skill 2 (3s cooldown)
  Deals 190–570% Physical Damage (scales up as your HP drops)
  Lifesteals based on Attack Power (scales up as your HP drops)
  Always hits and crits

  Applies Dauntless to self (12s):
  +15% Crit Chance · +25% Haste · -50% Mana Consumption

  Applies Decay + Cannot Heal to self (6s)
╰──────── HEALTH VAMP ────────╮
  50% proc on auto attack (no cooldown)
  Deals 80% True Hybrid Damage
  Heals you for 125% of base damage dealt
╰──────── PENITENCE ────────╮
  +25% Damage Resistance
  -25% DoT Boost
