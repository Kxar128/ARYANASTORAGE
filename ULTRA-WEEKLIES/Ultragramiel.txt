  Trigger: "The Grace Crystal prepares a defense shattering attack!"

  Trigger: "Gramiel attempts to drain your power… break his guard!"
  --> Everyone switches to attack Gramiel (mid boss)

SAFE STRAT (UPDATED)

3 Million HP ---> Boss Takes 0 damage ---> Starts taking damage again
---> wait 4 - 5 seconds

DPS taunts ---> Wait until "YOU DARE CHALLENGE ME EVIL DOER"

Call for LoO Taunt --> Half Taunt left
Call for SC Taunt --> Half Taunt left
Call for LR Taunt --> rush to 750 Thousand HP

--> Kill boss no need to taunt anymore
