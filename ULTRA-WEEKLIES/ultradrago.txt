ULTRADRAGO

TAUNTING AND SEALING:

Bow: Spam taunt as LR. Bow shouldn't live long enough to do anything

Axe: AP and LoO loop taunt. AP taunts first and then SEALS(AP skill 4) after taunting first. DO NOT BREAK SEAL (DO NOT PRESS SKILL 5) until the seal aura is almost gone. Seal(AP SKILL 4) off cooldown.


Normal Comp :

Decide who wants to DPS and who wants to Taunt, Enhancement Equipments will differ. 

(if you're going dps, please use pots)

CLASSES:

LR - Arcana's Concerto
AP - Lacerate
LoO - Awe Blast
DPS - CaV, CSS, AI, K.E

**

Scroll of Enrage for taunts

Boss Mechanism :

Don't focus Drago (he can't take damage until you kill right and left boss)
Axe Boss - On the Left side when you go into the boss room, you'll find 3 enemies, AP taunts the axe mob on left or insta party nuke

Bow Guy - LR taunts this boss on the right side of room and DPS focuses him before changing focus to left boss
