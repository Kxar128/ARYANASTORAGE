CHAMPION DRAKATH

Champion Drakath
♦ Every 2,000,000 dmg, Drakath will scream at your team, designated Taunter should taunt JUST BEFORE the 000,000 mark or else your team will not want to help xd
♦ Everytime he screams, he gains "Evasion" to self, increasing Dodge by 20%, stacks indefinitely.
♦ Recommended to have dual taunters if the DPS is CSS or DoT due to very fast damage
♦ Dual taunters take turns in first phase (first 10 million HP) and then only 1 takes next phase (whichever class has the best survivability (usually Stonecrusher, Obsidian Paladin Chronomancer, Archpaladin, Chaos Avenger, King's Echo)
