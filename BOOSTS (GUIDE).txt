📋 HOW BOOSTS WORK
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Boosts of the same kind do NOT stack — the game uses the highest equipped
  Example: Cape of Awe (25% Rep) + Dark Summoner armour (15% Rep) = 25% Rep total

  However, consumable boosts, server boosts, and gear boosts all stack with each other
  💡 Best time to farm: align a server boost + consumable boost + gear boost simultaneously

  ──────── Damage Boosts ────────
  Two boosts of the same type don't stack, BUT:
  --> A boost to All CAN stack with a boost to a specific tagged enemy type
  Example: Void Warlock Armour (25% All) + BLoD (75% Undead)
           = 118.75% extra damage vs Undead, 25% vs everything else

  ──────── Why 118.75%? (Multiplicative Stacking) ────────
  Boosts are calculated multiplicatively, not additively:
  --> 1.25 × 1.75 = 2.1875 = 118.75% extra damage

  ⚠️ Damage boosts do NOT affect DoTs or HoTs
