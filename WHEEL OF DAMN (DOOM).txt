   WHEEL OF DOOM

╚════════════════════════════════════╝

📋 OVERVIEW
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  The Wheel of Doom is essentially a loot box, located @ /join doom

  ──────── How to Spin ────────
  · Buy Fortune Tickets for 200 ACs each (1 ticket = 1 spin)
  · Obtain 3 Gears of Doom (log in daily, up to 3 per week) and spin for free
  · Member: click Legend Spin for a free daily spin

  ──────── Guaranteed Rewards Per Spin ────────
  · 1+ Treasure Potion(s)     redeemable in the Merge Shop
  · 1hr XP Boost              tradeable for other boost types in Merge Shop
  · 10,000 Gold

  ──────── Possible Bonus Rewards ────────
  · Bonus Item                includes cosmetics and the Alpha DOOMmega class
  · EIoDA (0.1% drop rate)    redeem for most items in the game
    💡 Trade 1,000 Treasure Potions for a WIoDA in the Merge Shop (same effect)
    💡 First 6 IoDAs grant a bonus IoDA each — then every 3rd IoDA after that
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

💡 TREASURE POTION RATES
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Amount depends on your Bonus Item progress
  (check Wheel Progress in your online account manager)

  Spin awards a Bonus Item                          ×1 TP
  Spin awards no Bonus Item                         ×2 TPs
  Spin tried to award a Bonus Item you already own  ×3 TPs
  Spin after all Bonus Items collected              ×6 TPs
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

💎 IoDA SUGGESTIONS
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  No time limit on redemption — take your time and choose wisely

  ──────── Functional ────────

  Legion Revenant
  --> Very well-rounded class — good choice if you don't want to farm endgame items
      Don't recommend other classes unless you're a collector
      (see Rare classes list for collector options)

  Bright Paragon
  --> Best Legion Token farming pet if you're fully committed to Legion
      Faster than the Shogun Paragon (best non-Rare method)

  Oblivion Blade of Nulgath
  --> Best for Nation fans — makes many reagents very fast
  ⚠️ Requires membership to access quests and "Assisting..." from Tendurrr

  Crag & Bamboozle
  --> Only pick this if you're F2P and never spend on membership or ACs
      Alternative to OBoN for F2P Nation farming

  Necrotic Sword of Doom
  --> Only worth it if you're non-mem and never want to farm endgame content
      💡 Alternatively: IoDA Sepulchure's SDKA → farm NSoD → farm Hollowborn DoomKnight
         (not possible if you IoDA NSoD directly)

  ──────── Cosmetics ────────

  Collection Chests
  --> Released during seasonal events, go Rare after
      Can contain 20–100+ cosmetic items
      See full collection chest list

  Limited Quantity Shop
  --> Recurring shop with cosmetics in limited supply — rarer over time
      New LQS appear in account manager
      💡 Worth checking older LQS too — see 2011–12 full list

  Quibble Shop
  --> Appears during Frostval, TLAPD, and story releases
      All items are Rare — large selection available
      See full Quibble list

  All Rare Shops
  --> Full list available

  Hardcore Paragon Pet
  --> Not great for farming LTs but has a quest with 1% drop cosmetics:
      Living BladeMaster · Katanas · Lesser Caladbolg
      Requires long-term Binky farming
