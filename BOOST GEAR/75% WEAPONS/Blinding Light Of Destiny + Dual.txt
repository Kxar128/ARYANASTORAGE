⚔️ STEP 1 QUESTS
╭──────── MERGE REQUIREMENTS ────────╮
  "Reforging the Blinding Light"
  Blinding Light of Destiny Handle ×1 --> /join doomwood (Arryd's shop)
  NOTE: If shop is locked, defeat 100 enemies in the top-left room of /doomwood
        to earn the "Reinforced" badge first (kill-counter top-right, don't leave)

  "Secret Order of Undead Slayers"
  Bonegrinder Medal ×1 --> /join doomwood (Arryd's shop - same as above)

  "Essential Essences"
  Undead Essence ×1,000 --> /join battleunderb (Kill enemies - also drops Bone Dust)

  "Bust some Dust"
  Bone Dust ×5,100 --> /join battleunderb (Kill enemies)

  "A Loyal Follower"
  Spirit Orb ×100  --> Complete "Essential Essences" and "Bust some Dust" until reached
  --> Then defeat Ephemerites at /join timevoid (First room to the right - 2 spawn there)

╔════════════════════════════════╗
              STEP 2
╚════════════════════════════════╝

🏆 MAIN FARM - 10,500 SPIRIT ORBS
╭──────── MERGE REQUIREMENTS ────────╮
  METHOD 1 (Lower level / less movement)
  Farm both "Bust some Dust" and "Essential Essences" from Artix at /join necropolis
  Turn in both quests when Undead Essence reaches 1k stack limit

  METHOD 2 (Faster - Yara's "Soul Searching" quest from /join battleunderc - 75 Undead Essence per turn-in)
  Exit left from /join battleunderc --> Walk up ladder --> Kill Undead Champion
  /join battleunderb --> Exit top-right --> Kill Bone Terror at /join battleundera
  Member: Exit left from /join battleunderd --> Kill Crystalised Jellyfish
  Non-member: Walk to it from /join battleunderc
╰────────────────────────────────────╯

⚒️ DAILY METAL QUEST (Complete each day while farming)
╭──────── MERGE REQUIREMENTS ────────╮
  --> /join necropolis --> Artix's "Mine Crafting" daily quest
  --> Kill Balboa at /join stalagbite
  Recommended picks: Copper, Barium, Silver (1 of each)
  Optional extras: 1 Iron (optional route), 4 extra Barium (for NSoD), 1 Aluminium (for ENNH)

  Upgrade each metal via Artix's corresponding quest (except NSoD and ENNH metals):
  Example for Copper - "Celestial Copper Enchantment":
    Undead Energy  ×25  --> /join battleunderb
    Spirit Orb     ×5   --> Complete "Bust some Dust" and "Essential Essences"
    Paladaffodil   ×25  --> /join arcangrove (Kill Seed Spitters - exit downwards from /natatorium)

  Before Step 3 you need:
  10,500 Spirit Orbs | 1 Celestial Copper | 1 Blessed Barium | 1 Sanctified Silver
  Optional: 1 Immortal Iron
