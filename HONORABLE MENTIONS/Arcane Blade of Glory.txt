╔════════════════════════════════════╗

   ARCANE BLADE OF GLORY

╚════════════════════════════════════╝

📦 HOW TO ACCESS
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  /join river --> Click the Chest in room 2
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

✅ STEP 1 — Obtain the Enchantment Rune
╭──────── "Victory Blade Enchantment" from Loot Chest NPC ────────╮
  Silver Victory Blade          ×1     --> Loot Chest NPC shop @ /join river
  --> Complete the quest to receive an Enchantment Rune as the reward
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

✅ STEP 2 — Forge the Enchanted Victory Blade
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Exchange in Loot Chest NPC shop:
  Enchantment Rune              ×1     --> Reward from Step 1
  Silver Victory Blade          ×1
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

✅ STEP 3 — Choose Your Path
╭──────── "Alignment Quest: Light... or Dark?" from Loot Chest NPC ────────╮
  🌟 For Arcane Blade of Glory  --> Choose Bright Aura Gem
  🌑 For Shadow Blade of Despair --> Choose Dark Aura Gem

  Exchange in Loot Chest NPC shop:
  Bright Aura Gem               ×1     --> Reward from this quest
  Enchanted Victory Blade       ×1     --> Reward from Step 2
  --> Produces the Enchanted Mana Blade
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

✅ STEP 4 — Final Merge
╭──────── "Choose: Despair... or Glory?" from Loot Chest NPC ────────╮
  Exchange in Loot Chest NPC shop:
  Amulet of Glory               ×1     --> Reward from this quest
  Enchanted Mana Blade          ×1     --> Reward from Step 3
  --> Produces the Arcane Blade of Glory
╰──────────────────────────────────╯
