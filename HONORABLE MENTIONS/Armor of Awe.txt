📦 PRE-REQUIREMENTS
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Level 55 (non-members only — required for Cape, Helm, and Armour)

  Recommended: Escherion's 13LoC Chapter Completion
  --> Unlocks optimal farming areas
      Access via Map > Story

⚔️ PART 1 — BLADE OF AWE
╭──────── Awe Quests from Valencia @ /join museum ────────╮
  Talk to Valencia > Artifact Quests > Armour of Awe > To the Crossroads

  "Find the Stonewrit!"
  --> Low level:    Sketchy Dragon @ /join j6 (970 HP, lower drop rate)
  --> Otherwise:    Greenguard Dragon @ /join greenguard (13.8k HP, higher drop rate)

  "Find the Handle!"
  --> Elementals @ /join gilead (walk down from Fire Elemental to find 3)

  "Find the Hilt!"
  --> Skeletal Warriors and Vikings @ /join castleundead
  ⚠️ Non-members: repeat until Rank 10 Blade of Awe rep
     (required to farm Cape, Helm, and Armour)

  "Find the Blade!"
  --> Hydra Heads @ /join hydra
      After Escherion's chapter: talk to Dew Drop fairy @ /join mobius
      > select The Hydra

  "Find the Runes!"
  --> Escherion @ /join escherion (usually others fighting already)

  ──────── After All Quests ────────
  Return to Valencia at the Crossroads > Forge the Blade > Create Blade of Awe

  💡 Unlocks Awethur's Accoutrements from the Awe-some Gear shop
     (+25% XP, CP, Gold, and Rep)
  💡 Unlocks Awe Enhancements shop with special procs:
     · Spiral Carve    (15%) — high damage + 25% crit boost for 10s
     · Awe Blast       (30%) — medium damage + −15% enemy crit/def for 10s
     · Health Vamp     (50%) — medium damage + HP restore
     · Mana Vamp       (80%) — low damage + restores 10 MP
     · Powerword Die   (0.3%) — 999,999 damage (too rare to be reliable)
  💡 See the enhancement guide for best choice per class

⚔️ PART 2 — CAPE, HELM, AND ARMOUR
╭──────── Valencia @ Crossroads in /join museum ────────╮
  Select Armour of Awe > open the Awe Passes shop
  Members:      Purchase both Legendary Awe Pass + Armour of Awe Pass
  Non-Members:  Purchase Armour of Awe Pass only

  How it works:
  Enemies drop Shards --> Turn in quest to convert to Fragments
  --> Merge Fragments into a Relic (Hero or Legend merge shop)
  --> Use Relic to merge the Cape, Helm, or Armour

  💡 Access each boss quickly via More Details in your Hero/Legend section
  👥 All bosses recommended in a group

  ──────── CAPE ────────
  "Cape of Awe"
  --> Defeat Binky @ /join doomvault ×1
  ⚠️ Difficult boss — group strongly recommended
     · Stuns ranging from 5–21 seconds
     · Skill Lock — bait with Mana Vamp enhancement, a scroll, or least-used ability
     · Prophetic Vision (reflects damage back) — press ESC to avoid
  💡 If you have the Hardcore Paragon Pet, accept its "A Single Rib" quest too

  ──────── HELM ────────
  "Helm of Awe"
  --> Defeat Undead Raxgore @ /join doomvaultb ×50
  ⚠️ Must complete quests at /join doomvault > /join doomvaultb to access
     (/goto does not work)
  ⚠️ Similar to Binky — can lock abilities and use Counter Attack
     Target off by clicking the X on the portrait or pressing ESC

  ──────── ARMOUR ────────
  "Pauldron of Awe"
  --> Defeat Ultra Akriloth @ /join gravestrike ×225 (Shards stack to 150)
  ⚠️ Complete the non-member (Hero) version — member version is daily-locked
      Requires Armour of Awe Pass

  "Breastplate of Awe"
  --> Defeat Carnax @ /join aqlesson ×100

  "Vambrace of Awe"
  --> Defeat Ultra Blood Titan @ /join bloodtitan ×225 (Shards stack to 150)
  ⚠️ Hits fairly hard, ~400k HP

  "Gauntlet of Awe"
  --> Defeat Ultra Alteon @ /join alteonbattle ×125 (Shards stack to 100, not 100% drop)
  ⚠️ Use /join alteonbattle — /join ultraalteon is significantly harder

  "Greaves of Awe"
  --> Defeat Mutated Void Dragon @ /join bosschallenge ×150 (not 100% drop, stacks to 150)
  ⚠️ Hits very hard, 400k+ HP
