╭──────────────────────────────────╮

XP (Experience Points)
--> Level up (Max Level: 100)

CP (Class Points)
--> Rank up classes (Max Rank: 10)

Reputation (Rep)
--> Rank factions (Good, Evil, Chaos, etc.)

Gold
--> Main currency (Cap: 100M)

Adventure Coins (ACs)
--> Premium currency
💡 Free ACs: January, October, December

╭──────────────────────────────────╮
