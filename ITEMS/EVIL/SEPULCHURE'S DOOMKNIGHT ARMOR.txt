╔════════════════════════════════╗

  SEPULCHURE'S DOOMKNIGHT
       ARMOR (SDKA)

╚════════════════════════════════╝

📦 PRE-REQUIREMENTS
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Membership (Required)
  Rank 5 Evil (Required)
  10+ Spare Inventory Slots      --> Buy for 200 ACs each (Recommended)

  Storyline Completion (Required):
  --> Nukemichi's quests at /join akiba
  --> Quests at /join bludrut > /join bludrut2 > /join bludrut3

  13 Lords of Chaos Story (Recommended):
  --> Makes navigating to required locations much easier

  Blinding Light of Destiny (Recommended):
  --> Many enemies are Undead · SDKA is a similar farm — good to learn together
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

╔════════════════════════════════╗
              STEP 1
╚════════════════════════════════╝

╭──────── Complete Vayle's quests at /join necropolis ────────╮

  "Descent into Darkness"
  ╭──────────────────────────────────╮
    Defeat Bellhops at /join necrodungeon
    --> Walk into either the left or right elevator
  ╰──────────────────────────────────╯

  "Dark Spirit Orbs"  (complete until you have ×40)
  ╭──────────────────────────────────╮
    Shadow Creeper Enchant         --> /join bludrut2 (Kill Shadow Creepers)
                                       Walk left then down to find 2
    Shadow Serpent Scythe          --> /join bludrut4 (Kill Shadow Serpents)
                                       Walk left to find 2
    Shadow Whiskers        ×6      --> /join ruins (Kill Dark Witch)
  ╰──────────────────────────────────╯

  "Sepulchure's Armour"
  ╭──────────────────────────────────╮
    Turn in ×40 Dark Spirit Orbs (from "Dark Spirit Orbs" above)
    DoomKnight Hood                --> Purchase from Dusk at /join shadowfall
    💡 Bank the Experimental Dark Item — you'll need it at the very end
  ╰──────────────────────────────────╯

  "The Doom that Looms"
  ╭──────────────────────────────────╮
    Rank 10 DoomKnight             --> Purchase from Dusk at /join shadowfall
                                       Requires Rank 10 Warrior and Healer
                                       (see XP section of New Player guide to rank these up)
  ╰──────────────────────────────────╯

  "Toiling with Terror"
  ╭──────────────────────────────────╮
    Shadow Terror Axe              --> /join battleundera (Kill Bone Terror)
                                       Exit top-right of /join battleunderb
    Elders' Blood                  --> Complete Reens' "Experiment 107: Elders' Blood Potion"
                                       quest from /join arcangrove
                                       (Go up into tower > blue portal at bottom-right)
                                       Defeat Gorillaphants at bottom-right (2 on same screen)
    ⚠️ This quest is only done once — it unlocks the next quests
  ╰──────────────────────────────────╯

  "A Penny for your Foughts"
  ╭──────────────────────────────────╮
    Defeat enemies at /join maul · DoomCoins stack to 100
  ╰──────────────────────────────────╯

  "Dark Spirit Donation"
  ╭──────────────────────────────────╮
    Farm "A Penny for your Foughts" for ×100 Dark Spirit Orbs
    💡 Shadow Creeper Enchants that drop can be used to complete "Dark Spirit Orbs"
       alongside this quest — or just stay at /join maul to avoid moving

    Dark Skull                     --> /join necrocavern (Kill Shadow Imp)

    ✅ After completing this quest, Dark Energy will drop from dark/evil enemies
       Use ×50 Dark Energy to merge ×1 Dark Spirit Orb from Vayle's shop
       OR save ×10,000 Dark Energy for Hollowborn DoomKnight
  ╰──────────────────────────────────╯
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

╔════════════════════════════════╗
              STEP 2
╚════════════════════════════════╝

╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Main goal: Obtain ×10,500 Dark Spirit Orbs
  Farm via "A Penny for your Foughts" + "Dark Spirit Orbs" (when Creeper Enchant drops)

  ✅ Complete Vayle's "Hard Core Metals" daily quest at /join necropolis every day
     --> Defeat Balboa at /join stalagbite
     --> Pick: Arsenic, Chromium, and Rhodium (×1 each)
     --> Also get ×1 Palladium (optional route at end of farm)

  For each metal obtained, complete its upgrade quest from Vayle:
  Example — Arsenic: "Accursed Arsenic Hex"
  ╭──────────────────────────────────╮
    Dark Energy            ×26     --> /join bludrut4 (Kill Shadow Serpents alongside
                                       "Dark Spirit Orbs")
                                       OR /join dwarfhold (Kill Chaos Drows in room with 3)
    Dark Spirit Orb        ×5      --> Farm "A Penny for your Foughts"
    Deadly Knightshade     ×16     --> /join arcangrove (Kill Seed Spitters)
                                       Exit downwards from /join natatorium
  ╰──────────────────────────────────╯
  Then turn in the metal with its upgrade quest · All metals require the same drops

  ─────────────────────────────────
  Before Step 3, you should have:
  ×10,500 Dark Spirit Orbs
  ×1 Accursed Arsenic
  ×1 Calamitous Chromium
  ×1 Reprehensible Rhodium
  ×1 Pernicious Palladium (optional)
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

╔════════════════════════════════╗
              STEP 3
╚════════════════════════════════╝

╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Open Vayle's merge shop at /join necropolis:
  1. Merge ×10,500 Dark Spirit Orbs --> ×105 Corrupt Spirit Orbs
  2. Merge ×100 Corrupt Spirit Orbs --> ×2 Ominous Auras

  Then go to Geopetal at /join dwarfhold (keep walking right)
  --> Forge the Accursed Arsenic of Doom in her shop

  Return to Vayle and complete the following quests:

  "Accursed Arsenic of Doom"
  ╭──────────────────────────────────╮
    Forge Key                      --> /join dwarfhold (Kill Albino Bat)
  ╰──────────────────────────────────╯

  "DoomSquire Weapon Kit"  (complete ×3)
  ╭──────────────────────────────────╮
    Skeletal Warriors              --> /join castleundead (left room has 2)
    War Mummies                    --> /join sandcastle (walk up until you see 2)
    Horc Noobs                     --> /join noobshire (walk right)
    Bronze Draconians              --> /join lair (exit left of /join underlair)
    Scarecrows                     --> /join farm (walk left)
    Rock Elemental                 --> /join bludrut (exit up ladder from /join bludrut2)
    Dark Makai                     --> /join nulgath or /join tercessuinotlim
  ╰──────────────────────────────────╯

  "DoomSoldier Weapon Kit"  (complete ×3)
  ╭──────────────────────────────────╮
    Stone Golem                    --> /join cornelis (keep going upstairs)
    Dai Tengu                      --> /join hachiko (select from Braeus' Fast Travel
                                       at /join shadowfall or /goto someone)
    Leatherwings                   --> /join pines (walk down then right to find 2)
    Vampire Knights                --> /join lycanwar (walk right to find 2)
    Tomb Robber                    --> /join sandport (walk ahead to find 2)
    Shadow Imp &
     Shadowstone Elemental         --> /join necrocavern
    Shadow Vordred                 --> /join vordredboss (attack during cutscene
                                       using long-range class, spam Next at top-right)
    Copper Sky Pirate              --> /join anders
  ╰──────────────────────────────────╯

  "DoomKnight Weapon Kit"  (complete ×4, plus extras if Ominous Auras don't drop — need ≥3)
  ╭──────────────────────────────────╮
    Sneeviltron                    --> /join boxes (walk right)
    Kitsune                        --> /join kitsune (invincible in fox form)
    Chaos Sphinx                   --> /join sandcastle (end of map)
    Protosartoium                  --> /join crashsite (walk right then top-right)
    Shadow Dragon                  --> /join necrocavern (select Necrocavern from Braeus'
                                       Fast Travel at /join shadowfall then walk down)
    Moganth                        --> /join dragonplane (walk up from Earth Elementals
                                       then follow the path)
    Shadow Nukemichi               --> /join akiba (complete Nukemichi's quests if inaccessible)
    Dark Wyvern                    --> /join dreamnexus (no screen with 2)
  ╰──────────────────────────────────╯

  ─────────────────────────────────
  Before Step 4, you should have:
  ×1 Accursed Arsenic of Doom
  ×3 Squire Weapon Kits
  ×3 Soldier Weapon Kits
  ×4 Knight Weapon Kits
  ×3 or more Ominous Auras
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

╔════════════════════════════════╗
              STEP 4
╚════════════════════════════════╝

╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  At Vayle's merge shop (/join necropolis):
  Merge Daggers of Destruction --> Shadow Daggers --> Necrotic Daggers of Destruction

  Complete "Pinpoint the Pieces with Necrotic Daggers" quest from Vayle
  --> Rewards ×30 Ominous Auras
  --> Farm Chaos Drows at /join dwarfhold (walk right until you find 3) for speed
  --> DoomKnight Armour Pieces stack to ×60
  💡 If you receive a Doom Aura, keep it for the final step (1% drop rate)

  Then do the following:

  Calamitous Chromium of Doom    --> Forge at Geopetal's shop at /join dwarfhold
                                     (farm "DoomKnight Weapon Kit" from Step 3 if low on materials)
  Merge ×25 Auras --> ×1 Diabolical Aura
                                 --> Vayle's merge shop at /join necropolis
  Merge Broadsword of Bane --> Shadow Broadsword --> Necrotic Broadsword of Bane
                                 --> Vayle's merge shop
  Complete "Pinpoint the Pieces with Necrotic Broadsword"
                                 --> Rewards ×1 Diabolical Aura
  Reprehensible Rhodium of Doom  --> Forge at Geopetal's shop at /join dwarfhold
  Merge Bow of the Shadows --> ShadowBow --> Necrotic Bow of the Shadows
                                 --> Vayle's merge shop

  ─────────────────────────────────
  Before Step 5, you should have:
  Necrotic Daggers of Destruction
  Necrotic Broadsword of Bane
  Necrotic Bow of the Shadows
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

╔════════════════════════════════╗
              STEP 5
╚════════════════════════════════╝

╭──────── Vayle's "Summoning Sepulchure's Armour" quest at /join necropolis ────────╮
  ╭──────────────────────────────────╮
    Corrupt Spirit Orb     ×250    --> Necrotic Bow (×10 "Pinpoint" runs)
    Dark Spirit Orb        ×500    --> Necrotic Bow
    Ominous Aura           ×125    --> Necrotic Daggers (×25 "Pinpoint" runs)
    Diabolical Aura        ×75     --> Necrotic Broadsword (×75 "Pinpoint" runs)
    Doom Aura              ×1      --> All Weapons (100% drop from Necrotic Scythe)
                                       OR farm weapons above (1% drop rate)
    Experimental Dark Item ×1      --> Saved from Step 1
    Heart of Darkness      ×1      --> /join ruins (Kill Dark Elemental — keep walking right)
    DoomKnight Weapon Kit  ×1      --> Leftover from Step 3
  ╰──────────────────────────────────╯

  "Pinpoint" run totals needed:
  --> Bow ×10 · Daggers ×25 · Broadsword ×75

  If the Doom Aura doesn't drop within these runs:
  Option A: Keep farming the "Pinpoint" quests (1% drop rate)
  Option B: Merge the Necrotic Scythe of Scourge (100% drop rate)
            --> Requires Pernicious Palladium of Doom + one of each Weapon Kit from Step 3

  ✅ Turn in "Summoning Sepulchure's Armour" to Vayle to claim SDKA
