        LEGION TOKENS

╚════════════════════════════════╝

💡 Legion Tokens stack to 25,000

⚔️ FARMING METHODS
╭──────── BRIGHT PARAGON — Fastest ────────╮
  Complete its quests by defeating enemies at /join brightfortress
  Best room: Commander Yulgar + 2 BrightScythe Reavers
  ⚠️ Bright Paragon pet is no longer available — only relevant if you already own it
╰──────── SHOGUN PARAGON — Fast (Best for most players) ────────╮
  Purchase Shogun Paragon pet during Dage's Birthday event (March–April)
  for 2,000 LTs from /join futurewardage

  Farm its "Time for Some Spring Cleaning" quest
  --> Kill Fotia Spirits and Elementals at /join fotia
  --> Drops stack to 50
╰──────── DREADROCK — Slow ────────╮
  Purchase Undead Champion armour from Dage's Legion shop at /join underworld
  --> Accept "Undead Champion Recruitment" from quest board (2nd screen at /join dreadrock)
  --> Kill enemies left of the quest board
╰──────── LEGION ARENA — Slowest ────────╮
  Complete "First Class Entertainment" from /join legionarena
  --> Kill Legion Fiend Rider (4% drop rate)
  💡 Can be more efficient than Dreadrock with a group of Chrono classes + supports

  Alternatively: Arcane Paragon quest (requires endgame player + Chrono class)

  Other Paragon pets worth using until Shogun:
  Festive · UW3017 · Mounted · Dreadnaught · Holiday · Thanatos · Ascended
  Regular · Infernal Caladbolg
  ⚠️ Hardcore Paragon is not viable to farm
  Only Bright Paragon is worth prioritising over Shogun
