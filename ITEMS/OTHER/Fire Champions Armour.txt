   SHADOW BLADE OF DESPAIR

╚════════════════════════════════════╝

📦 PRE-REQUIREMENTS
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Level 30 (required to equip)
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

✅ STEP 1 — Obtain the Enchantment Rune
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  1. Purchase the Silver Victory Blade
     --> Loot Chest NPC's shop @ /join river

  2. Complete "Victory Blade Enchantment" from the Loot Chest NPC
     --> Defeat Skeletal Vikings and Warriors @ /join castleundead
     --> Defeat Big Jack Sprat @ /join graveyard
     --> Rewards an Enchantment Rune

  3. Exchange the Rune + Blade for the Enchanted Victory Blade
     --> Loot Chest NPC's shop @ /join river
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

✅ STEP 2 — Choose Your Path
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Complete "Alignment Quest: Light... or Dark?" from the Loot Chest NPC
  --> Defeat Skeletal Warriors @ /join castleundead

  🌑 Shadow Blade of Despair  --> Choose Dark Aura Gem
  🌟 Arcane Blade of Glory    --> Choose Bright Aura Gem

  💡 This choice only affects appearance — see the image preview to decide
  Exchange the Aura Gem + Blade for the Enchanted Shadow or Mana Blade
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

✅ STEP 3 — Final Upgrade
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Complete "Choose: Despair... or Glory?" from the Loot Chest NPC
  --> Defeat Skeletal Vikings @ /join castleundead
  --> Rewards the Blade + Amulet of Despair or Glory

  Exchange both items to purchase your chosen weapon:
  · Shadow Blade of Despair
  · Arcane Blade of Glory

  💡 Both weapons grant a 20% bonus XP per kill and mission completion
  ⚠️ Requires Level 30 to equip
