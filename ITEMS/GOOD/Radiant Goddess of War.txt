🛡️ Braindead 50% All-Race Boost Armor Guide

Provides 50% damage boost to every race + resource boosts.
Only 10% below the maximum possible boost in the game (75% × 35%).

The grind isn't as bad as it looks, since many requirements are things most endgame players already have.

📋 Recommended Prerequisites

You should already have most of these if you’ve done endgame content:

• Fire Champion's Armor
• Drakath Armour (Empowered Version required for quest)
• Awescended
• Blinding Light of Destiny
• Rank 10 Good
• Malgor the ShadowLord (assuming you completed Dauntless)
