╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Level 100
  Rank 10 Evil
  Sepulchure's DoomKnight Armour (SDKA)
  Necrotic Sword of Doom (NSoD)

  Lae's Hardcore Contract + Hollowborn Shadow of Fate:
  --> Complete the Classic Hollowborn Paladin guide up to the last quest
      You need the Hollowborn Shadow of Fate for Quest 4 of this farm

  Sepulchure's Original Helm:
  --> Go to Gravelyn at /join shadowfall > select Sepulchure's Helm
  --> With SDKA in inventory, select Shop and purchase for free

  Storyline Completion:
  --> Complete all quests from /join doomvault > /join doomvaultb
  --> Optional (but recommended): /join noxustower > /join lightguardwar
      > /join lumafortress (can /goto someone but better to have done it yourself)
