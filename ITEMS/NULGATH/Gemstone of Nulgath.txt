   GEMSTONE OF NULGATH

╚════════════════════════════════════╝

📦 PRE-REQUIREMENTS
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Level 45
  Rank 10 Vampire
  ⚠️ This pet's quests are member-only
     Only farm as a non-member if you plan to get membership later
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

⚔️ STEP 1 — Obtain the Pet
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  1. Complete Valencia's quests @ /join crashsite until you have DwaToken III
  2. Head to /join crashruins and complete Valencia's quests until DwaToken X
     --> Keep DwaToken X in your inventory
  3. Farm 100 Dwobo Coins from one of Dwobo's quests:

     Non-Member  --> "Earn Dwobo Coins"
     Member      --> "More Dwobo Coins"

  👥 Kill route (both quests):
     --> Cluckmoo Idol at end of map
     --> Room right: 2 Unlucky Explorers
     --> Room down: 1 Spacetime Anomaly
  💡 Use a class that handles high-HP enemies to avoid swapping
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

⚔️ STEP 2 — Earn the Gemstone Receipt
╭──────── "Demanding Approval from Nulgath" (pet quest) ────────╮
  Complete to obtain the Gemstone Receipt of Nulgath

  Unidentified 13               ×4     --> Get 4; 1 needed again in Step 3
  Voucher of Nulgath            ×1
  Voucher of Nulgath (non-mem)  ×1
  Essence of Nulgath            ×100
  Totem of Nulgath              ×1

  Ultimate Darkness Gem         ×5
  --> /join shadowfallwar > walk up > right
      Defeat 2 Ghouls and 2 Undead War Mages
      Stacks to 2,500 — farm extras now for later

  Receipt of Nulgath            ×1
  --> Complete Nulgath's "Receipt of Nulgath" @ /join tercessuinotlim
      (or ENNH if owned). If no drop within 13 tries:
      trade Shredded ARS Documents at Swindle's shop

      Blade of Affliction        ×1  --> Buy from Nulgath's shop for 1.5m Gold
      Essence of Nulgath         ×10 --> Defeat Dark Makai @ /join nulgath
      ArchFiend's Favour         ×100
        --> /join evilwarnul — first room with grass (4 enemies)
            or farm start of map while doing Diligas' "Crush the Weak"
      Coal                       ×15
        --> /join extinction — room with 3 Cyworgs and 2 Control Panels
      Dwobo Coin                 ×10 --> See Step 1

  💡 Use the Reagent Library for farming details on all items above
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

⚔️ STEP 3 — Farm Gemstone Quests
╭──────── "Forge ___ for Nulgath" (pet quest) ────────╮
  Requires Gemstone Receipt of Nulgath to accept any forge quest
  Also needed for every quest:

  Unidentified 4 & 5
  --> 20% drop from "Supplies to spin The Wheel of Chance"
      Make sure they aren't blacklisted

  Unidentified 13               ×1     --> Reagent Library
  Hadean Onyx of Nulgath        ×1     --> Reagent Library

  ──────── "Forge Gemstones for Nulgath" ────────
  Balor's Cruelty               ×1  --> Defeat Abaddon @ /join twilight
  Yara's Sword                  ×1
  --> Complete Yara's "The Search for the Sword" @ /join battleundera
      Defeat enemies in the room left of Yara (3 enemies)
  Ultimate Darkness Gem         ×1  --> See Step 2

  💡 Use the Gems of Nulgath, Bloodstones, Quartz, and Tanzanite you earn
     to farm "Forge Dark Crystal Shards for Nulgath" and
     "Forge Blood Gems for Nulgath"
  ⚠️ The Diamonds and Tainted Gem quests are not worth farming
  💡 If more Gems of Nulgath are needed, see the Reagent Library

  If an Unidentified Gemstone of Nulgath drops:
  --> Use it for "Carve the Unidentified Gemstones"
      Also requires The Secret 1 (Reagent Library)
      Choose whichever reward you need
