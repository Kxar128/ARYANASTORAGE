Requirements

Storyline Completion
Complete the Memory of Nulgath's quests at /originul until you can reach the Fiend Champion, who you need to defeat until they drop the Yulgath's Hut house. You need the Hut to merge the Inn, which is an upgraded version.

Reagents

Merge this house with the Yulgath Hut in Dirtlicker's shop at /archportal.

Use the Reagent Library for details on how to get the following items:

Unidentified 10 x400

Tainted Gem x200

Dark Crystal Shard x250

Diamond of Nulgath x200

Unidentified 13

Voucher of Nulgath (non-mem)
