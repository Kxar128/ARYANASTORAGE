FASTEST

  - "The Dark Deal"

• Crag & Bamboozle AND Oblivion Blade of Nulgath

• "Bamboozle vs Drudgen"
• "The Dark Deal"

"Assisting Crag & Bamboozle / Drudgen" — Tendurrr
