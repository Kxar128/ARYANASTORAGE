📜 METHODS
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  1.
    Choose Reward - "Contract
    Exchange"
    Source: Drudgen
    Must own Crag & Bamboozle and
    Drudgen.

  2.
    20% - "Supplies to spin The Wheel
    of Chance" with "Bamboozle vs
    Drudgen" AND "The Dark Deal"
    Source: Crag & Bamboozle AND
    Oblivion Blade of Nulgath

  3.
    20% - "Supplies to spin The Wheel
    of Chance" with "Bamboozle vs
    Drudgen" OR "The Dark Deal"
    Source: Crag & Bamboozle OR
    Oblivion Blade of Nulgath

  4.
    50% - "New Worlds, New
    Opportunities"
    Source: Nulgath's Birthday Gift

  5.
    40% - "Nulgath (Larvae)"
    Source: Nulgath Larvae

  6.
    20% - "Supplies to spin The Wheel
    of Chance"
    Source: Drudgen or Swindle

  7.
    Choose Reward - "Voucher Item:
    Totem of Nulgath"
    Source: Drudgen
    You need 60 Essence of Nulgath
    from Dark Makai at
    /tercessuinotlim or /nulgath. Do
    this if it's the only item you
    need as you will get Gems of
    Nulgath and other useful reagents
    from the above methods.

  8.
    Alternative: Merge - Swindle's
    Ripoff Emporium
    Source: Swindle Bilk at
    /tercessuinotlim
    Do this if it's the only item you
    need as you would also obtain
    other reagents as you farm the
    above quests.

  9.
    Alternative - Choose Reward - "The
    Refreshing Deal"
    Source: Crag & Bamboozle
    Do this if you have spare
    Unidentified 10s and Blood Gems of
    the Archfiend.
