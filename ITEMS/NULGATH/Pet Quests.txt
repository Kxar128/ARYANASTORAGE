🎂 NULGATH'S BIRTHDAY GIFT
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Worth getting, but skip if you already own Crag & Bamboozle
  (unless you want maximum efficiency)
  --> Available for 1,500 ACs during Nulgath's birthday event (Jan–Feb)

  ──────── "New Worlds, New Opportunities" ────────
  Slugfit and Fire Imps   --> /join mobius
  Big Bad Boar            --> /join greenguardwest (exit down from /mobius)
  Cyclops Warlord         --> /join faerie (end of map, far right)
                              💡 Place an alt at this boss to /goto
  Dark Makai              --> /join tercessuinotlim or /join nulgath (member)

  💡 Pets with the same/similar quest: Bounty Hunter Drone, Astral Hanzo Orb,
     Crimson Hanzo Orb, Bounty Hunter Blade, Ceremonial Void Knight Sword
     All treated as "Nulgath's Birthday Gift" for simplicity
     Enemy locations may vary slightly per pet

🐛 NULGATH LARVAE
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Best overall farming quest for free players
  ⚠️ Not worth doing if you have Nulgath's Birthday Gift or Crag & Bamboozle

  To obtain:
  --> Requires membership
  --> Complete Drudgen's "Hire Nulgath Larvae" quest
      Voucher of Nulgath + Undead Legion Rune (see Reagent Library)
  --> Sometimes found equipped by players @ /yulgar-9889 (populated servers)
      Otherwise try /yulgar, /battleon, or /battleontown
  --> Also accessible via ENNH

  ──────── "Nulgath (Larvae)" ────────
  Mana Golem              --> /join elemental (stacks to 13)
  Mana Imps + Falcon      --> /join elemental (screen with 2 Imps + 1 Falcon)

🤝 DRUDGEN THE ASSISTANT
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Generally not worth buying — most quests are accessible without owning it
  Exception: "Contract Exchange" (requires Crag & Bamboozle to be useful)
  --> Buy for 5,000 ACs from Nulgath @ /join tercessuinotlim if desired
  --> Mobile version available for 2,000 ACs — not recommended

  Finding Drudgen without owning:
  --> Usually found @ /yulgar-9889 on populated servers
  --> Try /yulgar, /battleon, or /battleontown if no one around
  --> Accessible via Drudgen preview in Nulgath's shop or ENNH
  --> Swindle Bilk @ /tercessuinotlim also offers some quests

  ──────── Important Quests ────────

  "Contract Exchange"
  --> Trade Unidentified 13 + Blade Master Rune (from Shadow of Nulgath)
  --> Requires Crag & Bamboozle to farm efficiently
      (use "Diamond Exchange" to trade Diamonds for Uni 13s)

  "Supplies to spin the Wheel of Chance"
  --> Obtain a Relic of Chaos from /join escherion
      ⚠️ Escherion won't take damage while his staff is alive
      ⚠️ Must complete Galanoth's quests @ /join lair (incl. "Dragonslayer Reward")
         and Escherion's 13LoC saga to reach him (or /goto someone)
  --> 👥 Faster in a good group: farm Level 90 Hydras @ /join hydrachallenge
      Recommended classes: Lord of Order, Legion Revenant,
      ShadowStalker of Time, Chaos Slayer
  --> Ultra Alteon is viable but depends on group setup
      (fast-nuking calendar classes recommended)
  ⚠️ Don't farm Relics from Chaos Gemralds @ /join blindingsnow — drop rate too low
  💡 Hold onto Unidentified 1, 6, 9, 16, and 20
     → Trade at Swindle Bilk's "Swindle's Return Policy" @ /tercessuinotlim
  💡 Blacklist useless drops by shift-clicking in the custom drop UI

  "The Assistant"
  --> Turn in War-Torn Memorabilia from /join yulgar (Armour Shop)
  --> Faster than "Supplies..." but costs 100,000 Gold each time
  --> Used as substitute for "Supplies..." in most cases
      In some cases it's the better method (superior drop chance or
      exclusive drops not available from "Supplies...")

💎 CRAG & BAMBOOZLE
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Worth buying at 5,000 ACs if you're farming many Nation items
  Similar quests to Drudgen but faster — usable by you only
  --> Buy from Nulgath

  ──────── Important Quests ────────

  "Diamond Exchange"
  --> Trade 15 Diamonds of Nulgath + 1 Dark Makai Sigil for 1 Unidentified 13

  "Bamboozle vs Drudgen"
  --> Defeat Tainted Elemental @ /join evilmarsh or /join nulgath (member)
      Located at the very right of the map. Drops Tainted Cores (stacks to 13)
  --> Trade Tainted Cores for Relics of Chaos (stacks to 13)
      Use for "Supplies to spin the Wheel of Chance" much faster than normal
  --> 👥 Also earn Relics from Level 90 Hydras via "Bloody Chaos" quest
      (see Blood Gem of the ArchFiend section for details)
  💡 Hold onto Unidentified 1, 6, 9, 16, and 20
     → Trade at "Swindle's Return Policy" @ /tercessuinotlim
  💡 Blacklist useless drops by shift-clicking in the custom drop UI

  "GRUMBLE, GRUMBLE..."
  --> Daily quest: rewards 30 Diamonds of Nulgath
      5% chance to also drop Blood Gems for free
  💡 30 Diamonds = 2 Uni 13s via "Diamond Exchange"

  "The Poisonous Deal"
  --> Requires Tainted Claymore to accept
      → Get it from Skew's "Warrior Claymore Blade Enchant" @ /tercessuinotlim
        (requires /join tavern > /join llama completion + Rank 8 Evil)
      → Farm Warrior Claymore Blade from Undead Berserkers @ /join marsh2
  --> Turn in: 2 Blood Gems of the ArchFiend + 30 Unidentified 10s
               + Undead Plague Spear (drops from Big Jack Sprat @ /join graveyard)

  "The Refreshing Deal"
  --> Same as "Poisonous Deal" but requires Purified Claymore of Destiny
      → Get it from Polish's "Purified Claymore" @ /join cragglerock
        (requires /join citadel completion + Rank 8 Good)
      → Farm Warrior Claymore Blade from Undead Berserkers @ /join marsh2
  --> Turn in: 2 Blood Gems of the ArchFiend + 30 Unidentified 10s
               + Undead Plague Spear (drops from Big Jack Sprat @ /join graveyard)

  "Assisting Crag and Bamboozle"
  --> Accepted from Tendurrr the Assistant (member-only pet, drops from Dark Makai)
  --> Requires Crag & Bamboozle to accept
  --> Max-stack everything except Voucher of Nulgath (non-mem)
      Also keep Elders' Blood for Void Highlord or Unidentified 19 for ArchFiend DoomLord
  --> Daily-locked by Sparrow's Blood (get from Reens' quest @ /join arcangrove)

🗡️ OBLIVION BLADE OF NULGATH
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Similar to Crag & Bamboozle — makes getting Escherion Helms very easy
  💡 If you have both pets, run their quests simultaneously
  ⚠️ Rare item — only obtainable via IoDA now

  ──────── Important Quests ────────

  "The Dark Deal"
  --> Defeat Tainted Elemental @ /join evilmarsh or /join nulgath (member)
      Very right of the map. Drops Tainted Souls (stacks to 13)
  --> Trade for Relics of Chaos (stacks to 13)
      Use for "Supplies to spin the Wheel of Chance" much faster than normal
  💡 Hold onto Unidentified 1, 6, 9, 16, and 20
     → Trade at "Swindle's Return Policy" @ /tercessuinotlim
  💡 Blacklist useless drops by shift-clicking in the custom drop UI

  "Assisting Oblivion Blade"
  --> Accepted from Tendurrr the Assistant (drops from Dark Makai)
  --> Requires OBoN to accept
  --> Max-stack everything except Blood Gems, Vouchers, Totems, and Uni 19
  --> Tendurrr also has a Crag & Bamboozle quest for Voucher of Nulgath (non-mem)

  Bloodfiends         --> /join underworld (go left until you can go directly
                          down, then go right to find 3)
