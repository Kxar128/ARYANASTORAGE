🛒 WHAT TO BUY
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Don't buy things that are easy to farm!
  The following are AC-only purchases worth considering:
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

╭──────── 1. Join Legion — 1,200 ACs ────────╮
  Provides many benefits, most importantly: new classes
  Complete everything within 24 hours to recover 1,080 ACs (net cost: 120 ACs)

  Available year-round (Legion Tokens):
  · Infinite Legion Dark Caster · Yami no Ronin · Legion Revenant

  Seasonal (Dage's Birthday, March–April):
  · LTs: Legion DoomKnight, Legion SwordMaster Assassin
  · ACs: Timeless Dark Caster, Immortal Dark Caster

  💡 Suggested for new Legion members:
     Infinite Legion Dark Caster (farming) + Legion DoomKnight (soloing)
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

╭──────── 2. Nation Pets ────────╮
  Worth getting if you plan to do Nation farms

  Nulgath's Birthday Gift        1,500 ACs
  --> One quest with great reagents — requires moving around
      Only accessible by you. Cheaper option

  Crag & Bamboozle               5,000 ACs
  --> Can be farmed in one spot. Makes Unidentified 13 farming easy
      Quests cannot be shared unlike Drudgen

  Drudgen the Assistant          5,000 ACs
  --> Most quests accessible by other players when equipped
      Best purchased AFTER Crag & Bamboozle for synergy
      ("Diamond/Contract Exchange", "Drudgen the Salesman")
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

╭──────── 3. LightCaster — 1,000 ACs ────────╮
  Not essential — restricted to Level 80+ with better options available
  Good for soloing and support at low cost (+ a bit of farming)
  Pre-80: use the downgraded LightMage version (pair with Mana Vamp)
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

╭──────── 4. ArchFiend — 2,000 ACs ────────╮
  Buffed 15 Jan 2022 — makes other farming classes largely unnecessary
  Best farming class to buy right away
  --> Purchase from Oblivion @ /join tercessuinotlim
  💡 Also farmable at Level 50+
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

╭──────── 5. Calendar Classes — 6,000 ACs ────────╮
  Expensive but among the most powerful classes in the game
  --> Purchase from Stryche @ /join heromart (Rewards > AC shop)

  ShadowWeaver of Time    2017 Key 2.0
  Most versatile — soloing, group DPS, ultra bosses, farming, and PvP

  Timeless Chronomancer   2019 Key
  Best soloing class — also great in groups (requires reading & practice)

  Chronomancer Prime      2011 Key
  Simpler than TCM but less versatile. Good for soloing and group DPS

  Interstellar Knight     2016 Key
  Better at soloing than CMP. Use supports like Arachnomancer/Dragon of Time in groups
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

╭──────── 6. Bank Pet — 2,000 ACs ────────╮
  Lets you access your bank anywhere — just press B if it's in your inventory
  Budget: Zorbak or Twilly pet (Game Menu > Shops > AC Gear)
  Aesthetic: wait for TLAPD (November) for seasonal cosmetic options
  Free via Bonus Package: Hollowborn ShadowRaptor · Necrotic Pirate Commander
                          Akiban Blademaster · Apocalyptic Lich King
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

╭──────── 7. Inventory Slots — 200 ACs each ────────╮
  Essential for many farms (e.g. Void Highlord requires ~15 free slots)
  Useful if you like carrying many cosmetics and classes
  💡 Free slots are rewarded near the end of each year
