⚙️ PREP — ABILITY LOCKING
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  Before the fight, pre-lock any useless abilities in your kit:
  --> Attack Ezrajal and leave the room before dying
  --> An ability is locked when it becomes unclickable OR the screen
      shows yellow text: "[ability] has been locked"

  💡 You can also lock your weapon enhancement by auto-attacking ONLY
╰──────────────────────────────────╯

⚠️ BOSS MECHANIC — COUNTER PHASE
╭──────────────────────────────────╮
  At some point the boss will begin countering ALL abilities
  --> Watch for the text prompt on screen
  --> STOP attacking immediately during this phase
  --> Focus on healing and surviving until the counter aura fades
  --> Resume attacking once it clears
